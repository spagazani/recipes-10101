tags: unclassified
title: "Playdough"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 cup flour"
      - "1/2 cup salt"
      - "1 cup cold water, add food colouring to the water"
      - "2 Tbsp vegetable oil"
      - "2 tsp cream of tartar"
instructions:
  - "Mix all ingredients in a large pot. Stir over medium heat until a dough ball is formed."
  - "Knead ball and allow to cool. Seal in an airtight container or Ziplock bag."
