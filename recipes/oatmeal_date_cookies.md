--- 
title: "Oatmeal Date Cookies"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 cup unsalted butter, room temperature"
      - "1/4 cup vegetable shortening"
      - "1 cup packed crown sugar"
      - "3/4 cup sugar"
      - "2 large eggs"
      - "1 tsp vanilla"
      - "2 cups flour"
      - "2 1/2 cups rolled oats"
      - "1 cup dried dates, chopped into 1/4” pieces"
      - "1/2 tsp baking soda"
      - "1/2 tsp salt"
      - "1/2 tsp cinnamon"
instructions:
  - "Preheat oven to 375F. Beat the butter and shortening with a wooden spoon or mixer until smooth."
  - "Add both sugars and beat until smooth. Beat in eggs, one at a time until incorporated. Beat in vanilla."
  - "Combine the remaining ingredients in a separate bowl."
  - "Gradually add dry ingredients to the butter mixture, mixing just until combined."
  - "Drop batter by the tablespoon 1 1/2 inches apart on an ungreased baking sheet."
  - "Bake until golden brown, about 12 to 14 minutes. Transfer cookies to wire racks to cool."
---
