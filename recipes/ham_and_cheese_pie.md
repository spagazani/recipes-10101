--- 
title: "Ham And Cheese Pie"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "3 eggs, beaten"
      - "3/4 cup milk"
      - "Salt"
      - "Pepper"
      - "1 cup chopped cooked ham"
      - "1 cup shredded cheddar cheese"
      - "1 unbaked pie shell"
instructions:
  - "Beat eggs with milk, salt, and pepper in bowl."
  - "Add ham and cheese."
  - "Pour into pie shell."
  - "Bake at 350F for 45 minutes."
  - "Stand for several minutes before serving."
---
