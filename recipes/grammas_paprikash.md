--- 
title: "Gramma's Paprikash"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "2 pounds stewing beef"
      - "1 28oz tin tomatoes"
      - "1 bunch of leeks"
      - "5 to 6 banana peppers"
      - "3 to 4 whole onions"
      - "Carrots"
      - "Green beans"
      - "Potatoes"
instructions:
  - "Place in pot and cover with just enough water. Add an onion and cook for approximately 1 hour."
  - "Place meat in roasting pan with approximately 1 1/2 liquid from boiling water plus every other ingredient."
  - "Cover and bake at 375F until meat is tender. Uncover and bake 20 minutes more."
---
