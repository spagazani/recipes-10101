--- 
title: "Meatball Stroganoff"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 lb ground beef"
      - "1/2 cup fresh breadcrumbs"
      - "6 Tbsp milk"
      - "1 Tbsp margarine, melted"
      - "1 tsp salt"
      - "2 tsp fresh minced onion"
      - "Dash nutmeg"
      - "Dash cloved"
      - "Dash pepper"
      - "1 Tbsp margarine"
      - "Cooking spray"
      - "2 Tbsp flour"
      - "1 14oz can beef broth"
      - "1/2 cup fat free sour cream"
instructions:
  - "Combine first 9 ingredients in a bowl; stir well. Shape into 24, 1” balls."
  - "Melt 1 Tbsp of margarine in a large skillet coated with cooking spray over medium heat. Add meatballs; cook 8 minutes or until browned, turning frequently."
  - "Remove meatballs from skillet; set aside and keep warm. Place flour in skillet. Gradually add beef broth, stirring with a whisk until blended."
  - "Cook over medium heat for 3 minutes, or until slightly thickened, stirring constantly."
  - "Remove from heat; stir in sour cream. Return meatballs to skillet; cook over medium heat for 3 minutes or until meatballs are thoroughly heated."
  - "Do not boil or sour cream will curdle. Serve over rice or egg noodles."
