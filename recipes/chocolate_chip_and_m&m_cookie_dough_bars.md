--- 
title: "Chocolate Chip And M&M Cookie Dough Bars"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1/2 cup butter, softened"
      - "3/4 cup light brown sugar, packed"
      - "1 tsp vanilla extract"
      - "2 cups all-purpose flour"
      - "1 can sweetened condensed milk"
      - "1 cup chocolate chips"
      - "1/2 cup mini-M&M's"
      - "1/2 cup peanut butter"
      - "1/2 cup milk chocolate chips"
instructions:
  - "Line an 8x8 inch pan with parchment paper."
  - "In a large bowl, mix together the butter, and brown sugar until fluffy. Add your vanilla until combined with the sugar mixture. Alternate your flour and sweetened condensed milk until combined."
  - "Using a spatula, mix in chocolate chips and M&M's."
  - "Press the cookie dough into the bottom of the pan. Cover with foil and refrigerate until firm."
  - "Combine the peanut butter and the chocolate chips in the microwave, stirring every 30 seconds until melted."
  - "Pour and spread over the top of the chilled dough and chill again until firm."
---
