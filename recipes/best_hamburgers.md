--- 
title: "Best Hamburgers"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 1/2 pounds lean ground beef"
      - "1/2 onion, finely chopped"
      - "1/2 cup shredded Colby Jack or Cheddar cheese"
      - "1 tsp soy sauce"
      - "1 tsp Worcestershire sauce"
      - "1 egg"
      - "1 envelope dry onion soup mix or beef bouillon and onion powder"
      - "1 clove garlic, minced"
      - "1 Tbsp garlic powder"
      - "1 tsp dried parsley"
      - "1 tsp dried basil"
      - "1 tsp dried oregano"
      - "Salt and pepper to taste"
instructions:
  - "Preheat grill for high heat."
  - "In a large bowl, mix together the ground beef, onion, cheese, soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, egg, onion soup mix, garlic, garlic powder, parsley, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Form into 4 patties."
  - "Grill patties for 5 minutes per side on hot grill, until well done. Alternatively, pan fry for 5 minutes per side until done. Serve on buns with your favourite condiments."
---
