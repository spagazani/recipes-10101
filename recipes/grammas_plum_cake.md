tags: unclassified
title: "Gramma's Plum Cake"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1/2 cup butter, room temperature"
      - "1/2 cup sugar"
      - "2 eggs"
      - "2 cups flour"
      - "3 tsp baking powder"
      - "1 tsp salt"
      - "1/2 cup milk"
      - "10 to 15 prune plums, pitted and sliced"
      - "1 cup sugar"
      - "1 tsp cinnamon"
      - "1/2 tsp nutmeg"
      - "Pinch of ground cloves"
      - "1/2 cup chilled butter"
instructions:
  - "Preheat oven to 350F. Grease and flour an 13x9” pan. Cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in eggs."
  - "Sift dry ingredients up to milk together. Beat half into creamed mixture, beat in milk, and beat in remaining flour mixture. Pour batter into pan and arrange plum slices on top. Bake 25 minutes."
  - "Cut topping ingredients together with a fork. Quickly sprinkle topping mix over plums and bake 10-15 minutes more."
