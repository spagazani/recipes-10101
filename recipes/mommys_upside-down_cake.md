--- 
title: "Mommy's Upside-Down Cake"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1/3 cup shortening"
      - "1 cup sugar"
      - "1 egg"
      - "1 tsp vanilla"
      - "1 1/2 cups flour"
      - "2 tsp baking powder"
      - "1/2 tsp salt"
      - "1 cup milk"
      - "3 Tbsp butter or margarine"
      - "1/2 cup brown sugar"
instructions:
  - "Apple: slice 3 peeled apples over the sugar mixture"
  - "Peach: arrange one 20oz can drained peach slices over the sugar"
  - "Pineapple: place pineapple rings over the sugar and place maraschino cherries in the centers of the rings"
  - "Rhubarb: increase sugar to 1/4 cup. Cover sugar mixture with 2 cups diced rhubarb"
  - "Preheat oven to 350F. Cream shortening and sugar together, add egg and vanilla. Beat until light and fluffy."
  - "Sift flour, baking powder and salt together. Add to the creamed mixture alternately with the milk. Beat well after each addition."
  - "Melt 3 Tbsp butter in an 8” square baking pan, or more to taste, sprinkle with brown sugar (add more if desired)."
  - "Place down fruit option of choice and then pour batter over top. Bake for 35 to 35 minutes. Makes about 9 servings."
---
