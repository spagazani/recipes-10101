--- 
tags: salads
title: "Our Favourite 7 Layer Salad"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1/2 head lettuce, shredded"
      - "6 to 7 sliced celery stalks"
      - "1 green pepper, diced"
      - "1 bunch green onions, chopped"
      - "1 cup frozen peas"
      - "1 cup Miracle Whip"
      - "1/4 to 1/2 lb mozzarella cheese, shredded"
      - "Bacon bits"
instructions:
  - "Layer ingredients in order listed above. Let stand overnight. Serve."
  - "Could add layers of sliced mushroom or cucumber."
