--- 
title: "Mom's Pancakes"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 1/2 cups flour"
      - "1 to 2 Tbsp sugar"
      - "3 tsp baking powder"
      - "1/2 tsp salt"
      - "1 beaten egg"
      - "1 3/4 cups milk"
      - "2 Tbsp vegetable oil"
instructions:
  - "Combine all of the dry ingredients and then add all of the liquid ingredients, beat only until combined. Makes about 12 four-inch pancakes."
---
