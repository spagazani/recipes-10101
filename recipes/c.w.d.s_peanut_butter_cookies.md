--- 
title: "C.W.D.'s Peanut Butter Cookies"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1/2 cup shortening"
      - "1/2 cup brown sugar"
      - "1/2 cup sugar"
      - "1 egg, lightly beaten"
      - "1/2 cup peanut butter"
      - "1 tsp vanilla"
      - "1 1/2 cups flour"
      - "1/2 tsp baking soda"
      - "1/2 tsp salt"
instructions:
  - "Grease pans, preheat oven to 350F."
  - "In a large mixing bowl, combine shortening and sugars. Cream mixture well with a wooden spoon. Add egg, peanut butter, and vanilla."
  - "Sift together remaining ingredients, add to shortening mixture. Mix well."
  - "Drop by spoonful on prepared pans. Flatten cookies with a fork dipped in cold water."
  - "Bake approximately 10 minutes."
---
