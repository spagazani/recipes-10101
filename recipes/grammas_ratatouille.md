--- 
title: "Gramma's Ratatouille"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "3 zucchinis depending on size"
      - "3 large tomatoes, chopped"
      - "2 onions, chopped"
      - "Few green peppers"
      - "2 cloves garlic"
      - "1/4 cup olive oil"
      - "2 tsp salt"
      - "1/2 tsp oregano"
      - "Dash of pepper"
instructions:
  - "Sauté garlic in oil in a large frying pan. Add tomato and when pulpy, add other vegetables. Fry, stirring 5 minutes"
  - "Add salt, oregano, and peer, then serve."
---
