tags: unclassified
title: "Haystack Crunchies"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 cup butterscotch chips"
      - "1 cup peanut butter chips"
      - "1/2 cup butter"
      - "1/2 smooth peanut butter"
      - "3 cups dry chow mein noodles"
      - "1 – 1/2 cups Rice Krispies cereal"
      - "1 oz white chocolate or semisweet chocolate, melted"
instructions:
  - "In heatproof bowl over saucepan of hot (not boiling) water, melt butterscotch and peanut butter chips, butter, and peanut butter, stirring often; remove from heat. Stir in noodles and cereal to coat well."
  - "Drop by heaping tablespoonfuls (15 mL) onto waxed paper-lined baking sheets. Refrigerate for 20 minutes or let stand for 30 minutes or until firm. With fork, drizzle with chocolate."
