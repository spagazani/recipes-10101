--- 
title: "Charlotte Aux Bleuets"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "2 pkg Raspberry jellyrolls"
      - "4 tsp gelatin"
      - "2 1/2 cups milk"
      - "1 tsp vanilla"
      - "8 egg yolks"
      - "1 cup sugar"
      - "1 cup whipping cream"
      - "1 cup sour cream"
      - "1 cup blueberries"
      - "Garnish"
      - "3/4 cup whipping cream"
      - "1 cup fresh blueberries"
instructions:
  - "Line side of 8” springform pan with paper that extends 1” above rim. Line bottom and sides with Swiss roll slices."
  - "Stir gelatin into 1/2 cup cold water; set aside."
  - "In heavy pan, heat milk just until bubbles form around edge of pan, stir in vanilla."
  - "In a bowl, beat yolks well, gradually beat in sugar. Stir in hot milk; return to saucepan."
  - "Cook, stirring over low heat for 10 minutes or until thick enough to coat the back of a wooden spoon. Be careful not to overcook."
  - "Stir in gelatin until dissolved. Refrigerate for about 45 minutes or until partially set, whisking often."
  - "Whip cream, then fold in sour cream. Fold into chilled egg mixture; fold in blueberries. Mixture should be thick enough so that berries don't sink to bottom. If necessary, refrigerate for 15 minutes longer."
  - "Spoon mixture into Swiss roll pan. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 8 hours, up to 24 hours."
  - "Remove from pan; place cake on serving platter. Whip cream and garnish top and base with berries and cream."
---
