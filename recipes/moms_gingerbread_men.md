--- 
title: "Mom's Gingerbread Men"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 cup butter or margarine"
      - "2/3 cup brown sugar"
      - "1/2 cup molasses"
      - "1 egg"
      - "3 1/2 cup flour"
      - "1 tsp baking powder"
      - "1 tsp salt"
      - "1 tsp ginger"
      - "1 tsp cinnamon"
      - "1 tsp allspice"
      - "1 tsp cloves"
instructions:
  - "Beat butter, sugar, and molasses until light and fluffy. Add egg then remaining ingredients until well combined."
  - "Form a ball and wrap. Place in refrigerator for 3 to 4 hours."
  - "Roll out and cut cookies. Bake at 350F on lightly greased pans for 12 minutes or until lightly browned."
---
