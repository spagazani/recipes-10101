--- 
title: "Frozen Caramel Mousse Cake"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 cup sugar"
      - "3/4 cup water"
      - "4 egg yolks"
      - "2 cups whipping cream"
      - "1 tsp vanilla"
      - "25 ladyfinger cookies"
instructions:
  - "In a deep saucepan, stir water and sugar over medium heat. Cook for about 4 minutes or until dissolved."
  - "Increase heat to medium high. Cover and bring to a full boil, about 1 minute. Uncover and boil for 9 minutes or until light amber, remove from heat."
  - "Wearing oven mitt and using a greased metal spoon, remove 1/4 cups of the caramel and make shards."
  - "Holding pan away; add 1/2 cup boiling water to caramel. Stir smooth and let cool to room temperature."
  - "Beat egg yolks for 2 minutes or until pale. Beat in caramel in a thin steady stream. Place bowl over simmering water and cook, stirring for 5 minutes, or until thick."
  - "Refrigerate for 30 minutes or until cool. Mix whip cream with vanilla; fold into 1/4 of the caramel mixture. Fold in remaining cream."
  - "Cut ladyfingers into 2 1/2” lengths, reserving ends. Stand lengths, cut side down and curved side toward centre around the side of a 9” springform pan. Crumble reserved ends and pack on bottom of pan."
  - "Pour in filling and smooth top. Freeze for at least 6 hours or until firm. Top with the caramel shards. Makes 10 to 12 servings."
---
