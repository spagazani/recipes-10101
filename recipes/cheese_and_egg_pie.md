--- 
title: "Cheese And Egg Pie"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 unbaked pie crust"
      - "3 cups Cheddar cheese"
      - "4 eggs"
      - "1 cup milk"
      - "1/2 cup heavy cream"
      - "3 Tbsp flour"
      - "1/4 tsp salt"
      - "1/8 tsp pepper"
instructions:
  - "Preheat oven to 375F."
  - "Sprinkle cheese into the bottom of the pie crust, set aside."
  - "In a medium-sized bowl, combine the eggs, milk, cream, flour, salt, and pepper and beat with a wire whisk until the mixture is smooth and incorporated."
  - "Pour mixture carefully over the cheese in the pie crust."
  - "Bake quiche for 40-50 minutes until the quiche is puffed, golden brown and set. Let stand for 5 minutes."
  - "Cut into wedges, serve, and enjoy."
---
