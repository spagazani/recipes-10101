--- 
title: "Oven Baked Risotto"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 Tbsp olive oil"
      - "1/4 cup finely chopped onion"
      - "3/4 cup short grain rice, Arborio"
      - "1/4 cup water"
      - "1/2 cup chicken broth"
      - "Salt and pepper"
      - "1 Tbsp butter"
      - "1/4 cup grated Parmesan"
      - "1 Tbsp parsley"
instructions:
  - "Preheat oven to 425F."
  - "In a pan heat oil over medium high heat. Add onion and cook until it softens, 2 to 3 minutes. Add rice and cook coating grains with oil, about 1 minute."
  - "Stir in water and cook until completely evaporated. Add broth, 1 1/2 cups water, 3/4 tsp salt and 1/8 tsp pepper."
  - "Bring to a boil, transfer to an ovenproof dish, cover and bake until most of the liquid has evaporated, about 25 minutes."
  - "Remove from oven, add another 1/2 to 3/4 cup water, the butter, cheese, and parsley, serve immediately."
---
