--- 
title: "Pizza Chicken"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "olive oil"
      - "4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts"
      - "2 egg whites, lightly beaten"
      - "1 cup dried plain breadcrumbs"
      - "1 Tbsp Italian Seasoning"
      - "1/2 tsp table salt"
      - "1 tsp olive oil"
      - "1 1/2 cups canned tomato sauce"
      - "1/2 cup shredded Mozzarella cheese"
      - "2 Tbsp grated Parmesan cheese"
instructions:
  - "Preheat oven to 350F. Coat large pan with olive oil."
  - "Pound chicken to 1/4-inch thickness."
  - "Place egg whites in a shallow bowl. In another shallow bowl, combine breadcrumbs with Italian seasoning and salt. Dip chicken in egg whites, turn to coat. Dip chicken in bread crumb mixture to coat."
  - "In a frying pan over medium-high heat, place olive oil. Add chicken and cook until lightly browned and no longer pink in the centre. About 4 minutes a side."
  - "Pour 1/2 cup of sauced in prepared baking dish, place chicken in pan. Pour remaining sauce evenly over chicken, sprinkle with both cheeses. Bake until chicken is cooked through, and cheese is bubbly, about 25 minutes. Serve with noodles."
