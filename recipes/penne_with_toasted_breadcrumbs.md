title: "Penne With Toasted Breadcrumbs"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 Tbsp margarine"
      - "3 cloves garlic"
      - "1/4 cup Italian breadcrumbs"
      - "1/4 cup fresh chopped parsley"
      - "1/2 tsp dried rosemary"
      - "3 3/4 cups hot cooked penne"
      - "1/4 cup Renee's light Caesar dressing"
      - "1/4 cup grated Parmesan"
instructions:
  - "Melt butter in a small skillet over medium heat. Add garlic; sauté 1 minute."
  - "Add breadcrumbs; sauté 2 minutes or until breadcrumbs are lightly browned."
  - "Remove from heat and stir in herbs. Combine pasta and salad dressing in a large bowl; toss gently."
  - "Add breadcrumbs and cheese; toss gently. Serve immediately."
