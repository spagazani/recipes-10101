--- 
title: "Mom's Savoury Pepper Steak"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 1/2 lbs beef round steak"
      - "1/4 cup flour"
      - "1/2 tsp salt"
      - "1/8 tsp pepper"
      - "1/4 cup shortening"
      - "1 28 oz can tomatoes"
      - "1 3/4 cup water"
      - "1/2 cup onions, chopped"
      - "1 small clove garlic, minced"
      - "1 Tbsp beef bouillon"
      - "1 1/2 tsp Worcestershire"
      - "2 green peppers"
instructions:
  - "Cut steak into 1/2” thick strips. Coat with a mixture of flour, salt, and pepper. Brown steak strips in a large skillet with shortening."
  - "Drain tomatoes, reserving liquid. Add reserved liquid, water, onions, garlic, and beef bouillon to meat."
  - "Cover and simmer 1 1/4 hours or until meat is tender. Uncover, stir in Worcestershire sauce."
  - "Cut peppers into strips and add to meat. Cover and simmer meat and green pepper for 5 minutes."
  - "If gravy is too thin, combine 1 to 2 Tbsp of flour with equal amounts of cold water. Stir into sauce."
  - "Cook and stir until thickened and bubbly. Add cup up drained tomatoes; cook 5 minutes more."
  - "Serve over hot cooked rice."
---
