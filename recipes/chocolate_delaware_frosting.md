--- 
title: "Chocolate Delaware Frosting"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 1/4 cup icing sugar"
      - "1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder"
      - "6 Tbsp unsalted butter, room temperature"
      - "1/4 cup milk, room temperature"
      - "1 tsp pure vanilla extract"
instructions:
  - "Sift sugar and cocoa into a mixing bowl. Beat in butter, milk, and vanilla on medium speed until smooth."
  - "Use immediately or refrigerate, covered up to 3 days."
---
