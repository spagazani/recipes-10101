--- 
tags: appetizers
title: "Bacon Potato Puff Bites"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "3 lbs bacon"
      - "4 pkg McCain Tasty Taters"
      - "Process cheese slices, not too thick"
instructions:
  - "Cook strips of bacon until lightly browned but still limp. Cut in half."
  - "Prepare potato puffs as directed by package."
  - "Cut cheese slices into thirds and wrap a strip around a hot potato puff."
  - "Wrap a piece of bacon around the cheese and secure with a toothpick."
  - "Broil in the oven until bacon is crisp. Makes 100 puffs."
