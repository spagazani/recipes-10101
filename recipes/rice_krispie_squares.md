--- 
tags: brownies and bars
title: "Rice Krispie Squares"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1/4 cup butter or margarine"
      - "5 to 6 cups Rice Krispies"
      - "40 regular or 4 cups mini marshmallows"
      - "1/2 tsp vanilla extract"
instructions:
  - "Melt butter in a 4L saucepan. Add marshmallows over low heat, stirring constantly until marshmallows are melted add vanilla and mix until well blended."
  - "Remove from heat and add Rice Krispies. Stir until well coated with marshmallow mixture."
  - "Press warm marshmallow mixture into greased pan. Cut into squares."
---
