--- 
title: "Vegi Balls"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1/2 white radish"
      - "2 medium carrots"
      - "1 egg"
      - "Some all-purpose flour"
      - "2 green onions"
      - "Lots of vegetable oil"
      - "Salt and pepper"
instructions:
  - "White radish peeled and shredded, add some salt, massage until water comes out. Drain."
  - "Carrots, peeled and shredded, mix with white radish, ass egg, flour, green onion, salt, and pepper. Mix into a batter-like mixture."
  - "Heat oil in a big frying pan, spoon the batter into the oil. Deep fry until golden."
---
