--- 
tags: "marinades and seasonings"
title: "Judy's Marinade"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "2 Tbsp olive oil"
      - "3 Tbsp sherry"
      - "3 Tbsp soy sauce"
      - "1 Tbsp brown sugar"
      - "1 tsp Dijon mustard"
      - "2 green onions"
instructions:
  - "Mix ingredients together and let meat soak for an hour or overnight."
