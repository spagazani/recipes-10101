--- 
tags: soups
title: "Aunty Trish's Get Better Soup"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "Water"
      - "1 whole onion"
      - "Celery leaves and heart"
      - "6 skinless chicken breasts"
      - "2 cups celery"
      - "2 cups carrots"
      - "1 1/2 cup pasta"
      - "1/2 jar chicken bouillon"
      - "2 Tbsp parsley"
      - "1 Tbsp lemon juice"
instructions:
  - "Fill a large pot with water to about 4” from the top. Put whole onion, celery leaves and chicken breasts into water and bring to a boil. Simmer for 90 minutes."
  - "Strain liquid, discard onion and celery and set chicken aside."
  - "Add chopped up celery and carrots and pasta. Cook for 15 to 20 minutes."
  - "Add chicken bouillon, diced chicken, parsley, lemon juice and salt and pepper to taste."
  - "Serve. Tastes even better the next day!"
