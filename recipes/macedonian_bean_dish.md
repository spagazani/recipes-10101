--- 
title: "Macedonian Bean Dish"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 19oz tin white kidney beans"
      - "1 19oz tin Romano beans"
      - "1 large onion, chopped"
      - "1 green pepper, chopped"
      - "2 cloves garlic, minced"
      - "1 cup stewed tomatoes"
      - "1 1/2 Tbsp olive oil"
      - "1/2 Tbsp salt"
      - "1 tsp paprika"
      - "1 tsp mint"
      - "1/2 tsp pepper"
instructions:
  - "Sauté onion, green pepper, and garlic in oil until soft."
  - "Add tomatoes, paprika and salt. Continue to cook for a further 10 minutes."
  - "Drain beans well and place in a casserole or roasting pan. Pour fried vegetables over and add enough water so that the beans are not quite covered"
  - "Sprinkle with pepper and mint, and then bake at 300F for about 1 hour."
---
