--- 
title: "Bella Braised Chicken"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 Tbsp margarine"
      - "2 onions, chopped"
      - "2 celery stalks, diced"
      - "1 carrot, diced"
      - "2 garlic cloves, minced"
      - "3 Tbsp flour"
      - "1/4 tsp pepper"
      - "6 4oz chicken breasts"
      - "1 Tbsp olive oil"
      - "1 15oz can diced tomatoes"
      - "1 1/2 cups chicken broth"
      - "2 Tbsp parsley"
      - "1 tsp dried thyme leaves"
instructions:
  - "Preheat the oven to 325F."
  - "In a large skillet, melt margarine. Add the onions, celery, carrot, and garlic; cook stirring as needed, until softened, about 5 minutes. Transfer mixture to a 3-quart casserole dish."
  - "In a big Ziploc, combine the flour and pepper. Add the chicken and shake to coat."
  - "In the same skillet, heat the oil. Add the chicken and brown on each side, about 2 minutes."
  - "Arrange the chicken on top of the vegetables. Return the skillet to the heat; add tomatoes, broth, parsley, and thyme."
  - "Cook scraping up all of the browned bits from the bottom of the skillet, until the liquid comes to a boil; pour over the chicken."
  - "Bake covered until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are tender, about 1 hour."
---
