--- 
tags: brownies and bars
title: "Turtle Cookie Bars"
ingredients:
  - section: "Crust"
    items:
      - "2 cups all-purpose flour"
      - "1 cup firmly packed brown sugar"
      - "1/2 butter, softened"
  - section: "2nd Layer"
    items:
      - "1 cup pecan halves or chopped pecans"
      - "2/3 cup butter"
      - "1/2 cup firmly packed brown sugar"
      - "1 cup milk chocolate morsels"
instructions:
  - "Preheat oven to 350F degrees."
  - "Combine first 3 ingredients in a mixing bowl. Beat at medium speed with an electric mixture until blended."
  - "Pat mixture firmly into an ungreased 13x9 inch pan."
---
