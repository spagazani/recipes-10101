--- 
tags: dips
title: "Judy's Taco Dip"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 tub of sour cream"
      - "1 brick of cream cheese (room temperature)"
      - "1 pkg of Ole El Paso Taco seasoning"
      - "Bag of tortilla chips"
      - "1 cup of shredded Cheddar cheese"
      - "2 jars of salsa"
      - "1-2 green onion(s)"
      - "1 tomato"
instructions:
  - "Mix the sour cream, cream cheese, and taco seasoning until smooth."
  - "Spread mixture into a deep dish and let chill for 2+ hours."
  - "Before serving, top with salsa, then chopped tomatoes, then chopped green onions and finally the shredded cheese. Layer each ingredient."
  - "Serve with tortilla chips."
---
