--- 
title: "Philly Appetizer Rolls"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 load of bread"
      - "8 to 10 strips of crisp bacon"
      - "3 slices big Spanish onion"
      - "2 cups sliced mushroom"
      - "1 pkg Philadelphia cream cheese"
      - "Melted butter"
instructions:
  - "Cut the edges of the bread and roll flat. Sauté onions in butter, then add mushrooms and sauté until soft. Let cool slightly."
  - "Add onions and mushrooms to package of cream cheese. Break up bacon and add it to the filling."
  - "Paste onto the bread then roll. Brush with melted butter and keep rolling until frying pan or oven."
  - "Cut into discs and serve."
---
