--- 
title: "Garbanzo Salad"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "2 19oz cans of chickpeas"
      - "2 tomatoes, peeled and seeded"
      - "1/4 cup green pepper"
      - "1/4 cup green onions"
      - "1 clove garlic, crushed"
      - "1/3 cup parsley, chopped"
      - "1/2 tsp marjoram"
      - "1/2 tsp salt"
      - "1/4 tsp pepper"
      - "1/4 cup oil"
      - "1 Tbsp red wine vinegar"
      - "1 Tbsp lemon juice"
      - "Black olives, optional"
      - "Bacon bits, optional"
instructions:
  - "Place all ingredients, except olives and bacon bits in a salad bowl."
  - "Toss well and chill at least two hours."
  - "At serving time, garnish with the olives and bacon bits."
---
