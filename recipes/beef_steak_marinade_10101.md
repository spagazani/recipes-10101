--- 
tags: "marinades and seasonings"
title: "Beef Steak Marinade 10101"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 cup onions, chopped"
      - "1 clove garlic, minced"
      - "3 Tbsp olive oil"
      - "3/4 cup soy sauce"
      - "2 Tbsp honey"
      - "1/2 cup olive oil"
      - "1/3 lime juice"
      - "1 tsp curry powder"
      - "1 tsp chili powder"
      - "1 tsp pepper"
instructions:
  - "Sauté onions and garlic in 3 tbsp of olive oil until tender."
  - "Add all other ingredients and mix well. Marinades 2 1/2 lbs of beefsteak."
