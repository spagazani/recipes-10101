tags: dips
title: "Southwest Salsa"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "4 cups chopped peeled tomatoes"
      - "1 cup chopped onion"
      - "3 cloves garlic, minced"
      - "1/2 cup chopped sweet red pepper"
      - "2 to 4 jalapeno peppers, seeded and minced"
      - "1/2 cup red wine vinegar"
      - "1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro"
      - "2 Tbsp orange juice"
      - "1 Tbsp lime juice"
      - "1 tsp sugar"
      - "1 tsp pickling salt"
      - "1/4 cup tomato paste"
instructions:
  - "Combine everything except the tomato paste in a large stainless steel or enamel saucepan. Bring to a boil over high heat, reduce heat and boil gently, uncovered for 30 minutes or until mixture is thickened, stirring occasionally."
  - "Stir in tomato paste and cook for 2 minutes. Remove hot jars from canner and ladle salsa into jars to within 1/2-inch of rim."
  - "Process for 20 minutes for half pint and pint jars as directed in the Shorter Time Processing Procedure. Makes 4 cups."
