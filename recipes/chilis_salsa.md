--- 
title: "Chili's Salsa"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 can tomatoes and green chilies"
      - "1 can whole canned tomatoes (including juice)"
      - "4 tsp jalapenos (canned, diced)"
      - "1/4 cup yellow onion"
      - "1/2 - 3/4 tsp garlic salt"
      - "1/2 tsp cumin"
      - "1/4 tsp sugar"
instructions:
  - "In a food processor, place jalapenos and onions and process for a few seconds."
  - "Add both cans of tomatoes, the salt, sugar, and cumin, and then process ingredients until well-blended, but not pureed."
  - "Place everything in covered container and chill for a few hours."
  - "Serve with corn tortilla chips."
---
