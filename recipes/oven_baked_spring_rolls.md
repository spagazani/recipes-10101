--- 
title: "Oven Baked Spring Rolls"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "2 garlic cloves, minced"
      - "2 Tbsp carrots, shredded"
      - "2 Tbsp onion, chopped"
      - "2 Tbsp celery, finely diced"
      - "2 Tbsp green bell peppers, strips"
      - "2 Tbsp red bell peppers, strips"
      - "2 Tbsp cabbage, shredded"
      - "1/4 tsp low sodium soy sauce"
      - "4 spring roll wrappers"
instructions:
  - "Preheat oven to 350F. Lightly coat skillet with cooking spray. Sauté garlic briefly and add carrots, onions, celery, peppers, and cabbage."
  - "Add soy sauce and continue to sauté until tender crisp, about 5 minutes. Remove from heat and let cool."
  - "Place 3 Tbsp cooked vegetables in each spring roll wrapper and roll. Lightly coat baking sheet with cooking spray and bake until lightly brown."
---
