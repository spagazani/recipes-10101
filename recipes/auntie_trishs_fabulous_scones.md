--- 
title: "Auntie Trish's Fabulous Scones"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "2 cups flour"
      - "1/4 cup sugar"
      - "1/2 tsp salt"
      - "2 1/2 tsp baking powder"
      - "1/2 tsp baking soda"
      - "1 cup milk"
      - "1 Tbsp vinegar"
      - "1/4 cup margarine"
      - "1/2 cup raisins"
instructions:
  - "Mix dry ingredients. Soften raisins in hot water. Drain and mix with flour. Cut in margarine."
  - "Mix milk and vinegar together, let it sour. Mix into flour mixture. Turn on floured board."
  - "Pat to 1/2” to 3/4” thick. Dough is wet and sticky. Knead it a bit. Flatten with hand. Cut with a floured glass."
  - "Sprinkle with 1 tsp sugar. Bake in 450F oven for 10 minutes."
---
