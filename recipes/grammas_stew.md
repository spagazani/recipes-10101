title: "Gramma's Stew"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 to 2 lbs stewing beef"
      - "1 cup flour"
      - "2 Tbsp butter"
      - "1 cup water, more or less"
      - "Worcestershire sauce"
      - "Gravy improver"
      - "1 onion, chopped"
      - "2 cups potatoes, cubed"
      - "1 cup carrots"
      - "1 cup celery"
instructions:
  - "Dredge meat liberally with flour. Brown meat in heavy pot with melted butter. When meat is browned, add water, Worcestershire sauce to taste, gravy improver and onion."
  - "Simmer for approximately 1 1/2 hours making sure enough water is in pot throughout cooking time and stirring to make sure stew does not burn."
  - "Add vegetables and simmer for about 20 minutes."
  - "Add Gramma's dumplings 20 minutes before you are ready to eat."
