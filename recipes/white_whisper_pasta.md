tags: unclassified
title: "White Whisper Pasta"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "2 tsp olive oil"
      - "2 cups sliced mushrooms"
      - "3 cloves garlic, minced"
      - "1/2 cup milk"
      - "1/3 cup non-fat cottage cheese"
      - "3 Tbsp basil leaves"
      - "3 Tbsp parsley leaves"
      - "2 Tbsp flour"
      - "1 tsp dried oregano"
      - "1/4 cup Parmesan"
      - "2 Tbsp Asiago cheese"
      - "4 cups fusilli"
      - "1/2 lb green beans, cut into 2-inch pieces"
instructions:
  - "Heat the oil in a large skillet. Add the mushrooms and garlic; cook stirring as needed, until the mushrooms release their liquid."
  - "Puree the milk, cottage cheese, basil, parsley, flour, and oregano. Add to the mushroom mixture, stir in the grated Parmesan and Asiago cheeses, and cook, stirring as needed, until heated through, about 2 minutes."
  - "Meanwhile, cook the pasta, adding the green beans during the last 4 minutes of your cooking."
  - "Drain and transfer to a large bowl. Immediately top with mushroom sauce; toss to coat."
