--- 
title: "Grasshopper Parfaits"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 qt vanilla ice cream"
      - "3 Tbsp white crème de cacao"
      - "3 Tbsp green crème de menthe"
      - "Whip Cream"
      - "1/2 cup chocolate wafer crumbs"
      - "1/2 cup chocolate syrup"
      - "Maraschino cherries"
instructions:
  - "Stir liqueurs well into ice cream."
  - "Layer ice cream and wafer crumbs in a parfait glass. Top with whipped cream and a cherry."
  - "Place in freezer until serving."
---
