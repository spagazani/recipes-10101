tags: unclassified
title: "Gramma's Dumplings"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 cup flour"
      - "2 tsp baking powder"
      - "1/2 tsp salt"
      - "3 Tbsp shortening"
      - "1/2 cup milk"
instructions:
  - "Blend or sift together flour, baking powder, and salt. With pastry blender, cut in shortening."
  - "Stir in milk, blending just until flour is moistened. Drop by large spoonsful on simmering stew."
  - "Cover pan tightly and cook for 15 minutes. Do not lift cover during cooking. Yield 6 dumplings."
---
