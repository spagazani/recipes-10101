--- 
title: "Peach Spice Cake"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 cup butter"
      - "1 1/2 cups sugar"
      - "1 tsp almond extract"
      - "2 eggs"
      - "2 cups flour"
      - "2 tsp baking soda"
      - "2 Tbsp cocoa powder"
      - "1 tsp cinnamon"
      - "1/2 tsp cloves"
      - "1/2 tsp allspice"
      - "1/2 tsp salt"
      - "2 cups heated peach pulp"
instructions:
  - "Cook ripe peaches on medium until they turn pulpy and juicy."
  - "Mix together all ingredients and bake at 350F for 25 to 45 minutes."
  - "Drizzle almond icing over top of cake and serve."
---
