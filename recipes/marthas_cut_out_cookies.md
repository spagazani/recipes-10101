--- 
title: "Martha's Cut Out Cookies"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "12 Tbsp unsalted butter"
      - "3/4 cup sugar"
      - "1 large egg yolk"
      - "1 tsp vanilla"
      - "2 cups flour"
      - "1/2 tsp salt"
      - "2 cups icing sugar"
      - "1 Tbsp cocoa powder"
      - "1/4 cup milk"
instructions:
  - "Preheat oven to 350F. Beat butter until it is light and fluffy. Add sugar and beat until combined. Beat in egg yolk and vanilla and then flour and salt."
  - "Pat dough into a disk between 2 sheets of saran wrap. Refrigerate for about 1/2 hour. Roll out dough to 1/4 inch thick."
  - "Using cookie cutters, cut out cookies and place onto an ungreased baking sheet. Bake for about 15 minutes, transfer to a rack and cool completely."
  - "For icings, put 2/3 cup icing sugar into three small bowls. Stir 4 tsp of milk into each bowl, adding cocoa into one and food colouring if desired into the third."
  - "Spread a little icing over each cookie and let harden for 20 minutes."
---
