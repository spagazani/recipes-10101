--- 
title: "Chocolate Chip Bars"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "6 Tbsp margarine, softened"
      - "1/3 cup sugar"
      - "3/4 cup firmly packed dark brown sugar"
      - "2 large egg whites"
      - "2 tsp vanilla extract"
      - "2 1/2 cups all purpose flour"
      - "1/2 tsp baking soda"
      - "1/8 teaspoon salt"
      - "1/2 cup semisweet chocolate mini chips"
      - "Cooking spray"
instructions:
  - "Preheat oven to 375F."
  - "Cream margarine and sugars at medium speed of a mixer until light and fluffy. Add egg whites and vanilla beat well."
  - "Combine flour, baking soda, and salt in a medium bowl, gradually add to creamed mixture, beating well. Stir in chocolate mini chips."
  - "Press cookie dough into bottom of a 13x9” baking pan coated with cooking spray. Bakr for 10 minutes. Let cook in pan."
---
