--- 
tags: chicken
title: "Farmer's Market Chicken"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "3 Tbsp tomato paste"
      - "1/4 tsp salt"
      - "1/4 tsp pepper"
      - "1 10oz can chicken broth"
      - "1 clove garlic minced"
      - "2 Tbsp Italian seasoned breadcrumbs"
      - "4 4oz chicken breasts"
      - "1 Tbsp olive oil"
      - "1 cup diced carrot"
      - "1 cup diced red onion"
      - "1 cup diced yellow squash"
      - "1 cup diced zucchini"
      - "2 Tbsp chicken broth"
      - "4 cups hot cooked long grain rice"
      - "2 tsp chopped fresh rosemary"
instructions:
  - "Combine the first 5 ingredients in a medium bowl; stir well with a whisk. Place breadcrumbs in a shallow dish and dredge chicken."
  - "Heat oil in a large skillet over medium high heat; add chicken, and sauté 6 minutes on each side."
  - "Remove chicken from skillet. Add carrot and next 4 ingredients to skillet: sauté 5 minutes."
  - "Return chicken to skillet; add broth mixture. Bring mixture to a boil; cover, reduce heat, and simmer 15 minutes."
  - "Serve chicken and vegetables over rice, sprinkle with rosemary."
