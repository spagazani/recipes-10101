--- 
title: "Mommy's Tuna And Macaroni Salad"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1/2 cup Miracle Whip"
      - "1/2 tsp sugar"
      - "2 Tbsp milk"
      - "2 cups cooked macaroni"
      - "1 7oz tin drained tuna"
      - "1/2 cup green pepper"
      - "1/8 tsp cayenne pepper"
      - "1/8 tsp garlic salt"
      - "Dash paprika"
instructions:
  - "Mix together first 3 ingredients. Add next 5 ingredients and mix well."
  - "Sprinkle with paprika and refrigerate until serving."
---
