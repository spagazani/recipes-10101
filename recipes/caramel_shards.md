--- 
title: "Caramel Shards"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 cup sugar"
      - "1/2 cup water"
instructions:
  - "In a deep saucepan, stir together over medium heat. Cook, stirring constantly for 4 minutes or until dissolved."
  - "Increase heat to medium high. Cover and bring to a full rolling boil, about 1 minute."
  - "Uncover and boil without stirring for about 7 minutes or until light amber. Remove from heat."
  - "Let cool slightly. Drizzle onto a greased baking sheet in lacy zig zag patterns."
  - "Let cool completely and break into pieces. Great for decorating the top of cakes."
---
