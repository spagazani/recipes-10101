tags: unclassified
title: "Onion Cheese Ball"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 pouch onion soup mix"
      - "2 cups sour cream"
      - "1 250g pkg cream cheese"
      - "2 cups grated cheddar"
      - "1/2 cup crushed soda crackers"
      - "1 cup chopped walnuts"
      - "Parsley flakes"
instructions:
  - "Prepare onion dip with soup mix and sour cream."
  - "Blend 1 cup of the dip, cream cheese, and cheddar until smooth. Add crackers and half of the nuts."
  - "Pack into foil lined bowl; chill. Unmold and cover with remaining nuts and parsley."
  - "Makes 3 1/2 cups. Remaining dip can be served with raw vegetables or chips."
