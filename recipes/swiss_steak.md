--- 
title: "Swiss Steak"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "4 beef cube steaks (4oz each) or cut up a Sirloin Roast"
      - "1 Tbsp canola/olive/becel oil"
      - "1 medium onion, chopped"
      - "1 celery rib with leaves, chopped"
      - "1 garlic clove, minced or 1/4 tsp garlic powder"
      - "1 can 28oz stewed tomatoes, diced"
      - "1 jar Tomato and Basil sauce"
      - "6 packets of beef bouillon"
      - "2 Tbsp cornstarch"
      - "4 Tbsp cold water"
instructions:
  - "In a large skillet, brown cube steaks on both sides in oil over medium high heat; remove and set aside."
  - "In the same skillet, sauté the onions, celery, and garlic for 3 to 4 minutes or until tender. Add the tomatoes, tomato sauce and bouillon."
  - "Return steaks to the pan. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 1 1/4 to 1 3/4 hours or until meat is tender."
  - "Combine cornstarch and water until smooth; stir into tomato mixture. Bring to a boil; cook and stir for two minutes or until thickened."
---
