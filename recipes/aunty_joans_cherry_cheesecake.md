--- 
tags: layered desserts
title: "Aunty Joan's Cherry Cheesecake"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 3oz pkg lemon Jell-O"
      - "1 cup boiling water"
      - "2 cups graham wafers"
      - "1/2 cup butter"
      - "1 Tbsp sugar"
      - "1 pkg Dream Whip"
      - "1 8oz pkg cream cheese"
      - "1 cup sugar"
      - "1 can cherry pie filling"
instructions:
  - "Mix Jell-O and water, permit it to start jelling. Mix graham crumbs, butter, and sugar into 9” square pan."
  - "Mix Dream Whip according to directions. Combine cream cheese, sugar, Dream Whip and Jell-O mixture together and beat for 10 minutes."
  - "Place on top of crust and place in freezer for a short while."
  - "Pour cherry pie filling over top and serve."
---
