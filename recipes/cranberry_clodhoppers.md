tags: unclassified
title: "Cranberry Clodhoppers"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "4 cups Golden Grahams crushed"
      - "1 tsp oil"
      - "2 cups vanilla wafer chocolate"
      - "1 cup salted peanuts"
      - "1 cup dried cranberries"
instructions:
  - "Melt oil with wafers for 1 minute and 45 seconds. Stir to ensure they are melted."
  - "Add the rest of the ingredients and stir to coat. Spray a non-stick cookie sheet with Pam."
  - "Spread mixture onto the cookie sheet evenly and refrigerate. Break and enjoy!"
