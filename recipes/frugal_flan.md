--- 
title: "Frugal Flan"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "4 Tbsp butter"
      - "1 cup chopped onion"
      - "1 cup sliced mushrooms"
      - "3 cups shredded cheddar cheese"
      - "2 cups cubed ham"
      - "2 nine-inch unbaked pie shells"
      - "4 eggs, beaten"
      - "2 1/2 cups milk"
      - "1/2 cup flour"
      - "1 tsp salt"
      - "1 tsp pepper"
      - "Tomato slices"
instructions:
  - "Preheat oven to 375F."
  - "Melt butter, add onions and mushrooms. Cook until tender. Sprinkle both with cheese and ham over bottom of pie shell."
  - "Combine eggs and milk. Gradually blend in flour, salt, and pepper. Pour into shells."
  - "Bake 30 to 40 minutes or until knife inserted comes out clean. Serve hot or cold garnished with tomato slices."
---
