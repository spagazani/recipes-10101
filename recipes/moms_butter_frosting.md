--- 
title: "Mom's Butter Frosting"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "3 Tbsp soft butter or margarine"
      - "1/2 tsp vanilla"
      - "Few grains of slat"
      - "2 cups sifter icing sugar"
      - "2-2 1/2 Tbsp warm cream or milk"
      - "Food colouring, optional"
instructions:
  - "Cream butter. Add in vanilla and salt. Blend in icing sugar alternately with warm cream."
  - "Beat until smooth and of spreading consistency. Add more icing sugar or milk if necessary."
  - "Add food colouring if using to decorate cake."
---
