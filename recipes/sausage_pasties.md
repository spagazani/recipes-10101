tags: unclassified
title: "Sausage Pasties"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "3 lbs sausage meat"
      - "2 large onions"
      - "Curry"
      - "Garlic powder"
      - "Paprika"
      - "2 pkgs prepared pastry – 4 pies"
instructions:
  - "Brown together meat and onions. Add seasonings to taste. Roll out pastry and using wine glass, cut out small circles."
  - "Put a little bit of filling in the centre of each circle. Fold each circle over into crescents and seal edges with a fork."
  - "Place on baking sheet and bake at 350F, watching carefully so that they do not burn."
