--- 
title: "Mom's Lasagna-Double Recipe"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "2 lbs ground beef"
      - "1 13oz can tomato paste"
      - "2 28oz cans tomatoes"
      - "2 tsp salt"
      - "2 tsp basil"
      - "2 tsp parsley"
      - "1 tsp rosemary"
      - "1/4 tsp garlic powder"
      - "Dash cayenne pepper"
      - "2 bay leaves"
      - "Lasagna noodles"
      - "Mozzarella and Parmesan cheese"
instructions:
  - "Brown ground beef, add next 9 ingredients and simmer 1 hour."
  - "Layer meat mixture with cooked noodles and cheeses in a 13x9” pan."
  - "Bake one hour at 375F."
---
