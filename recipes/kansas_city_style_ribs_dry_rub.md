--- 
title: "Kansas City Style Ribs Dry Rub"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "2 cups brown sugar"
      - "1/2 cup dry mustard"
      - "1 Tbsp cayenne pepper"
      - "1 Tbsp smoked paprika"
      - "1 Tbsp garlic powder"
      - "1 Tbsp onion powder"
      - "1 Tbsp salt"
      - "2 tsp pepper"
instructions:
  - "Combine all ingredients and massage into ribs."
