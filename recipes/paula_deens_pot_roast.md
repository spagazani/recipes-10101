--- 
tags: beef
title: "Paula Deen's Pot Roast"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "3 bay leaves"
      - "1 thinly sliced onion"
      - "1/4 cup vegetable oil"
      - "1 1/2 tsp House Seasoning"
      - "1 – 3lb chuck roast"
      - "3-4 beef bouillon cubes"
      - "2 cloves crushed garlic"
      - "1 – 10 3/4 oz can cream of mushroom soup"
      - "1/2 cup chardonnay wine or beef broth"
instructions:
  - "Sprinkle roast on all sides with house seasoning."
  - "Using a skillet over high heat, sear roast until brown in oil."
  - "Place roast in a pan, pour remaining ingredients over top, add 1 cup of water."
  - "Bake in a 350F oven for 1-2 hours or until roast is well done."
  - "Can be made in a crock pot. Add all ingredients, and enough water to cover everything. Cook for 8 hours on low setting."
