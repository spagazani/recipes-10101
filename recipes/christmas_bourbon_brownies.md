--- 
title: "Christmas Bourbon Brownies"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 1/2 cups flour"
      - "1/2 tsp baking soda"
      - "1/2 tsp salt"
      - "1 cup sugar"
      - "2/3 cup vegetable oil"
      - "4 Tbsp water"
      - "12oz chocolate chips"
      - "2 tsp vanilla"
      - "4 eggs"
      - "2 cups walnuts"
      - "8 Tbsp Rye"
      - "1 cup margarine"
      - "4 cups icing sugar"
instructions:
  - "Combine, sugar, oil, water and chocolate chips and melt. Add vanilla and eggs; mix well. Add sifted dry ingredients. Mix in walnuts."
  - "Bake at 325F for 30 minutes. Pour rye over cake after it has baked. Mix together margarine, vanilla and icing sugar to spreading consistency."
  - "Spread over cooled brownies, sprinkle with chopped walnuts."
---
