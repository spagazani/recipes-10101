--- 
tags: frostings
title: "Seven Minute Kaboom Frosting"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "3/4 cup sugar"
      - "1/8 tsp cream of tartar"
      - "Few grains of salt"
      - "3 Tbsp cold water"
      - "1 unbeaten egg white"
      - "1/2 tsp vanilla"
instructions:
  - "Combine everything with the exception of the vanilla, in the top of a double boiler."
  - "Place over rapidly boiling water and beat constantly with an electric mixer until the frosting is stiff enough to stand in peaks. This will take about 4 to 7 minutes."
  - "Remove from heat and fold in vanilla."
  - "This will frost an 8” or 9” cakes. Make sure to be careful! This recipe has been known to explode!"
