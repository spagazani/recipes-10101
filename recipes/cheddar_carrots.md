--- 
title: "Cheddar Carrots"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 1/2 lbs carrots"
      - "1/4 cup butter"
      - "1/2 cup onions, chopped"
      - "1 cup shredded cheddar"
      - "1/2 cup crushed saltines"
      - "1/2 tsp salt"
instructions:
  - "Cook sliced carrots in salted water until tender. Melt butter, add onions."
  - "Sauté until tender, add carrots and toss well. Combine cheese, cracker crumbs, salt, and pepper."
  - "Place in casserole dish in layers, carrots, cheese mixture, carrots, etc. Bake 30-35 minutes at 350F."
---
