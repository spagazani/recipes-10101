--- 
tags: chicken
title: "Lemon Bum Chicken"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 whole chicken about 3 1/2 lbs, rinsed and patter dry"
      - "2 garlic cloves, smashed"
      - "2 Tbsp butter, room temperature"
      - "Salt and pepper"
      - "1 lemon, washed and quartered"
      - "1/3 cup white wine or water"
      - "1/2 tsp thyme"
instructions:
  - "Preheat oven to 425F. Rub chicken all over with the garlic and then the butter. Season inside and out with 1 tsp salt and 1/2 tsp pepper."
  - "Place garlic and lemon inside the chicken's bum. Tie legs together with twine. Place chicken in a roasting pan and tuck the wing tips under."
  - "Roast until the juices run clear when thigh is pierced, 45 to 50 minutes. Transfer to a platter and carve."
  - "Use juices from pan, wine, 1/2 cup water and thyme. Bring to a boil in a small pan; cook until sauce thickens slightly, about 3 minutes. Serve sauce with the chicken."
