--- 
tags: brownies and bars
title: "Rice Krispies Crispy Fudge"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "2 cups semisweet chocolate chips"
      - "1/2 cup butter or margarine"
      - "1/2 cup corn syrup"
      - "2 tsp vanilla extract"
      - "1 cup icing sugar"
      - "4 cups Rice Krispies cereal"
instructions:
  - "Melt together chocolate chips, butter, and corn syrup over low heat. Remove from heat and add vanilla."
  - "Stir in icing sugar until smooth. Add cereal and mix until thoroughly combined."
  - "Spread into a lightly greased 13x9” pan. Chill until set. Cut into squares to serve. Makes about 48 squares."
---
