--- 
title: "Gramma's Butter Tarts"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "Pastry for 3 pies"
      - "4 eggs"
      - "4 cups brown sugar"
      - "4 Tbsp vinegar"
      - "2 tsp vanilla"
      - "1 cup melted butter"
      - "1 2/3 cups Thompson raisins"
      - "1 cup walnuts or pecans, chopped"
instructions:
  - "Beat eggs only until whites and yolks are well blended. Beat in sugar and vinegar and vanilla."
  - "Stir in melted butter and raisins and walnuts. Melt all ingredients in a pot until heated through."
  - "Line tart tins with pastry and fill 1/2 to 3/4 full with butter tart mixture."
  - "Bake in 450F oven for first 10 minutes then reduce temperature to 350 and bake for 20 to 25 minutes or until filling is firm. One recipe does enough tarts for the whole season."
---
