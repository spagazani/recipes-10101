--- 
title: "Hans And Patty's Going Bananas"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 1/2 cups sugar"
      - "3 eggs"
      - "3/4 cup oil"
      - "2 cups flour"
      - "2 tsp baking soda"
      - "1/2 tsp salt"
      - "1 tsp cinnamon"
      - "1 tsp allspice"
      - "2 cups mashed bananas"
      - "1/2 cup raisins"
instructions:
  - "Preheat oven to 350F. Grease and flour a bundt pan or two loaf pans."
  - "Beat sugar and eggs until creamy. Mix in oil."
  - "Sift flour, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, and allspice. Add to creamed mixture. Mix in bananas and raisins."
  - "Pour into bundt pan and bake for 1 hour or until done. Test with a toothpick, it should come out dry. Turn out of pan when cool."
---
