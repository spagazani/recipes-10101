--- 
title: "Gramma's Dark Fruit Christmas Cake"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "4 cups seedless raisins"
      - "2 cups currants"
      - "3 cups seeded raisins"
      - "3 cups candied cherries, halved"
      - "2 cups cut up dates"
      - "3 cups candied fruit"
      - "1 cup cut up candied pineapple"
      - "2 cups walnuts"
      - "2 Tbsp cut up candied ginger"
      - "10 cups flours"
      - "5 tsp baking powder"
      - "5 tsp cinnamon"
      - "3 1/2 tsp salt"
      - "1 2/3 tsp nutmeg"
      - "1 2/3 tsp ginger"
      - "1 1/2 tsp mace"
      - "1 1/2 tsp cloves"
      - "3 1/4 cups butter"
      - "4 1/4 cups brown sugar"
      - "20 eggs"
      - "7 to 8 cups molasses"
      - "1 1/3 cup cold strong coffee"
      - "Sherry, Kirsch, or another liqueur"
instructions:
  - "Mix fruit, nuts, and ginger. Pour Kirsch over fruit then sift flour etc.…over fruit."
  - "Cream butter, add sugar and mix until light and fluffy. Add fruit and flour mixture to butter mixture."
  - "Preheat oven to 300F, line pans with greased brown paper or parchment paper."
  - "Pour batter into prepared pan and bake for 3 to 3 1/2 hours."
  - "To mellow cake, cool completely. Wrap cake tightly in cheesecloth soaked in Kirsch or? for several weeks in an airtight container."
  - "To make sure that it turns out, have little hand help with the cutting and measuring of fruit."
  - "Also, for authenticity, be sure to leave out at least one ingredient and remember to add it while the cakes are in the oven!"
---
