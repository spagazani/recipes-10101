--- 
title: "Gramma's Meat Pies"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "Tenderflake, deep dish pie crusts"
      - "3 Tbsp margarine"
      - "3 to 4 small onions, thinly diced"
      - "3 to 4 lbs ground beef"
      - "4 pkg beef bouillon"
      - "1 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce"
      - "Flour and water mixture to thicken"
instructions:
  - "Melt margarine in a large saucepan. Sauté onions until tender. Add ground beef and cook until it is no longer pink. Add bouillon and Worcestershire sauce to taste."
  - "Mix flour and water together to make a paste, add to beef to make gravy. Spoon into pie crusts and top with additional pie crusts. Poke holes in top to allow for ventilation."
  - "Bake at 350F until top is nice and golden brown. Extra pies may be frozen for future use."
---
