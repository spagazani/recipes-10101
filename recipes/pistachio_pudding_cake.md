--- 
title: "Pistachio Pudding Cake"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 package Jell-O pistachio pudding"
      - "1 pkg 2 layer yellow or white cake mix"
      - "4 eggs"
      - "1 1/4 cups water"
      - "1/4 cup oil"
      - "1/2 tsp almond extract"
instructions:
  - "Blend pudding mix with cake mix. Add eggs, water, oil, and almond extract. Beat 2 minutes at medium speed."
  - "Pour into greased and floured 10” tube or bundt pan. Bake at 350F for 50 to 55 minutes or until tester comes out clean."
  - "Cool in pan 15 minutes. Remove from pan and finish cooling on rack. Drizzle Almond Icing over top and serve."
---
