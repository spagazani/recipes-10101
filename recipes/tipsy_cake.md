tags: unclassified
title: "Tipsy Cake"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 5oz banana cream pie filling"
      - "1 3/4 cups milk"
      - "1 pound cake mix"
      - "1 14oz tin fruit cocktail"
      - "1/3 cup rum"
      - "1 1/4 cups whipped cream"
      - "Glace fruit"
instructions:
  - "Make pie filling using the milk. Cover with wax paper and chill. Bake pound cake in 9” round tube pan. Cool."
  - "Mix rum with fruit cocktail and let it stand covered until cake and filling have cooled."
  - "Slice top off of cake and hollow out leaving 1” wall at sides and 1/2” at base."
  - "Drain fruit cocktail and mix together with pie filling. Soak cake with fruit juice and rum mixture."
  - "Fill hollowed out center with pie filling and replace top of cake. Cover with whipped whip cream."
