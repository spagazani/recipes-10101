--- 
tags: salads
title: "Gramma's Spinach Salad"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "10oz washed fresh spinach"
      - "1 tomato, cut in wedges"
      - "1 onion, red or Spanish"
      - "1/2 cup sliced mushrooms"
      - "6 cooked bacon slices, crumbled"
      - "1/3 tsp salt"
      - "1 clove garlic, minced"
      - "1 tsp dry mustard"
      - "1/4 tsp oregano"
      - "1/2 tsp pepper"
      - "1/2 cup olive oil"
      - "1 1/2 Tbsp lemon juice"
      - "1/3 cup olive oil"
      - "2 Tbsp red wine vinegar"
instructions:
  - "Combine first 5 ingredients in a salad bowl."
  - "Mix together next 5 ingredients and add your choice of lemon juice or red wine vinegar with the respective amount of oil. Chill and serve."
