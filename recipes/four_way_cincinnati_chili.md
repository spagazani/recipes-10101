--- 
tags: mains
title: "Four Way Cincinnati Chili"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "2 lbs ground beef"
      - "2 cups chopped onion"
      - "1 cup chopped green pepper"
      - "1 28oz can diced tomatoes"
      - "1 1/4 cups water"
      - "2 Tbsp chili powder"
      - "1 tsp ground cumin"
      - "1/2 tsp salt"
      - "1/2 tsp cinnamon"
      - "1/4 tsp allspice"
      - "1/4 tsp pepper"
      - "1 15oz can kidney beans, rinsed and drained"
      - "1 6oz can tomato paste"
      - "5 cups hot cooked spaghetti"
      - "10 Tbsp cheddar cheese"
      - "10 Tbsp chopped red onion"
      - "2 1/2 cups plain goldfish crackers"
instructions:
  - "Cook beef, onion, and green pepper in a large pot over medium high heat, until beef is browned and crumbled."
  - "Stir in diced tomatoes and the next 9 ingredients. Bring to a boil; cover, reduce heat, and simmer 30 minutes, stirring occasionally."
  - "Divide cooked 1/2 cup spaghetti among serving bowls. Spoon 1 cup chili over pasta; top each with 1 Tbsp of cheese and onion and 1/4 cup crackers."
