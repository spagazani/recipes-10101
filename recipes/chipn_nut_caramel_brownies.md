--- 
tags: brownies and bars
title: "Chip'N Nut Caramel Brownies"
ingredients:
  - section: "Batter"
    items:
      - "1/2 cup butter"
      - "1/2 cup semisweet chocolate chips"
      - "3/4 cup sugar"
      - "2 eggs"
      - "1 tsp vanilla"
      - "3/4 cup flour"
      - "1/2 tsp baking powder"
      - "1/4 tsp salt"
  - section: "Topping"
    items:
      - "1 277g pkg of caramels"
      - "2 Tbsp whipping cream or light cream"
      - "2/3 cup chopped pecans"
      - "3/4 cup semisweet chocolate chips"
instructions:
  - "Melt butter and chocolate chips in large saucepan, stirring until smooth. Remove from heat."
  - "Add sugar, eggs, and vanilla. Mix well. Add flour, baking powder, and salt, stirring until smooth."
  - "Spread batter in greased 8” or 9” square cake pan. Bake at 350F for 20 to 25 minutes or just until set."
  - "While brownie base is baking, heat caramels and cream on low heat stirring until smooth."
  - "Remove brownies from oven. Let cool 5 minutes. Spread caramel mixture on top. Sprinkle with pecans and chocolate chips."
  - "Bake 5 minutes longer. Cool completely and cut into squares. Makes about 25 squares."
