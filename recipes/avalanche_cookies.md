--- 
title: "Avalanche Cookies"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1/2 cup mini chocolate chips"
      - "16 ounces vanilla flavoured candy coating"
      - "1 (15oz) jar creamy peanut butter"
      - "2 cups Rice Krispies cereal"
      - "1 1/2 cups mini marshmallows"
instructions:
  - "Line 2 baking sheets with parchment paper. Set aside."
  - "Melt candy coating in the microwave, stirring every 30 seconds."
  - "Add Rice Krispies to a large bowl and set aside."
  - "Add peanut butter to candy coating and combine. Pour vanilla candy mixture over the Rice Krispies and allow mixture to cool to room temperature."
  - "Add marshmallows and chocolate chips, stir to combine."
  - "Use a 2 Tbsp to portion treats on baking sheet. Allow to set in fridge until firm."
---
