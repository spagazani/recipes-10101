--- 
tags: frostings
title: "Penuche Icing"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1/2 cup butter"
      - "1 cup brown sugar"
      - "1/4 cup milk"
      - "1 3/4 to 2 cups icing sugar"
instructions:
  - "Melt butter and add brown sugar. Boil and stir over low heat for 2 minutes."
  - "Add milk and bring to a boil, stirring constantly. Cool till lukewarm."
  - "Gradually add icing sugar. Beat until thick."
---
