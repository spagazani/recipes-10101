--- 
title: "Gold Medal Fajitas Marinade"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1/4 cup vegetable oil, divided"
      - "3 Tbsp lemon juice"
      - "1 garlic clove, minced"
      - "1 tsp cumin"
      - "1 tsp garlic powder"
      - "1 tsp onion powder"
      - "1 dash of hot sauce"
      - "Salt"
      - "Pepper"
      - "1 1/2 lbs meat (chicken breast, or skirt steak)"
instructions:
  - "In a heavy-duty resealable plastic bag, combine 3 tbsp of the vegetable oil, lemon juice, garlic powder, onion powder, hot sauce, salt, pepper, and your choice of meat."
  - "Seal and toss the bag around to coat. Marinate in the refrigerator for at least 20 minutes."
  - "Heat your pan to a high temperature, remove the meat from the marinate and place it in the pan, cook chicken until cooked through, about 5 minutes per side."
  - "Serve with sautéed onions, peppers, sour cream, salsa, guac, cheese and tortillas."
---
