--- 
title: "Carmen Newton's Spring Rolls"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "Shredded cooked chicken breast"
      - "Onion"
      - "Mixed vegetables"
      - "Fried cubed sweet potato"
      - "Shredded cabbage"
      - "Spring roll wraps"
instructions:
  - "Sauté onion and chicken breast. Then add cabbage and the mixed vegetables."
  - "Last to be added is the sweet potato. Add salt and pepper to taste."
  - "Put in a strainer and when the mixture is cold, roll it in the spring roll wrappers, then deep fry."
---
