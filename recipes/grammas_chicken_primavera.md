--- 
title: "Gramma's Chicken Primavera"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "24oz spaghetti"
      - "1/2 cup Parmesan"
      - "4 Tbsp butter"
      - "4 Tbsp vegetable oil"
      - "2 cloves garlic, minced"
      - "4 chicken breasts cut into 1/4” strips"
      - "4 cups broccoli pieces"
      - "2 cups chopped celery"
      - "4 carrots, sliced"
      - "1 cup sliced red pepper"
      - "6 chopped green onions"
      - "1 28oz tomato sauce"
      - "1/2 cup suage"
      - "1/2 cup white vinegar"
      - "1 Tbsp dry mustard"
      - "1 Tbsp Worcestershire"
      - "2 pouches chicken bouillon"
instructions:
  - "Cook spaghetti about 10 minutes, drain. Stir in cheese and butter."
  - "Meanwhile heat oil, sauté garlic and chicken until brown, stir in remaining ingredients."
  - "Cook over low heat 8 to 10 minutes, stirring occasionally. Spoon over spaghetti."
---
