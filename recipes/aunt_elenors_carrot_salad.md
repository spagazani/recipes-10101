--- 
tags: salads
title: "Aunt Elenor's Carrot Salad"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 lb cooked carrots"
      - "1/2 tin tomato soup"
      - "1/2 white sugar"
      - "1/2 salad oil"
      - "1/2 tsp salt"
      - "1/2 tsp pepper"
      - "1/2 tsp dry mustard"
      - "1/3 cup vinegar"
      - "1 green onion, cut into ringlets"
      - "1 large onion, cut into ringlets"
instructions:
  - "Boil everything except the carrots together for 5 minutes."
  - "Pour mixture over carrots and toss."
---
