---
tags: salads
title: "Amber's Yummy Salad"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1/3 cup olive oil"
      - "2 cups olive bread (ACE)"
      - "1 tsp Dijon mustard"
      - "2 Tbsp pure maple syrup"
      - "2 Tbsp white balsamic vinegar"
      - "2 Tbsp finely chopped red onion"
      - "1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen sherries, pitted and quartered"
      - "8 cups romaine lettuce"
      - "2 Tbsp roasted pumpkin seeds"
      - "1/2 cup feta"
instructions:
  - "To make croutons – heat 2 tbsp olive oil in a large non-stick skillet over medium heat."
  - "Add olive bread cut into chunks and sauté until golden, about 8 minutes, set aside."
  - "Whisk together remaining 1/4 cup olive oil, syrup, balsamic vinegar, and onions, set aside."
  - "Toss romaine lettuce with pumpkin seeds, feta, cherries, and croutons, then mix with dressing."
