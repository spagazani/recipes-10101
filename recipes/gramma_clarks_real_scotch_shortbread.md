title: "Gramma Clark's Real Scotch Shortbread"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 lb butter"
      - "4 cups all-purpose butter"
      - "1 cup fruit sugar"
instructions:
  - "Preheat oven to 300°F."
  - "Cream butter until fluffy. Add sugar gradually and mix until light."
  - "Add 3 1/2 flour and save remaining for counter"
  - "Turn out dough and gradually kneed in the flour."
  - "Pat down about 1/2” thick and scallop edges with fingers or knife handle. Prick all over with fork."
  - "Place in oven on sheet tray(?) for 35-40 minutes. Cut with pizza cutter as soon as it comes out of the oven."
