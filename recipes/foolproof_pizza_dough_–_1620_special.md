--- 
title: "Foolproof Pizza Dough – 1620 Special"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 cup warm water"
      - "1 Tbsp honey"
      - "1 Tbsp oil"
      - "1 Tbsp instant yeast"
      - "1 tsp salt"
      - "2-3 cups flour"
instructions:
  - "In a large bowl mix water, honey, oil, yeast, and salt. Add the flour gradually with a whisk until a soft dough forms. Add more flour as needed until you have a dough that is soft and smooth, not leaving residue on your fingers."
  - "Knead the dough for 5-6 minutes."
  - "Let the dough rest, covered for 10 minutes. Shape the dough into pizza, spread with sauce and toppings of choice. Bake at 475F for 8-10 minutes."
  - "NOTE**This recipe doubles, triples, and quadruples well. If doubling, use double the amount for all ingredients. If triple or quadruple batches increase all ingredients except the yeast. Only use 2 Tbsp for a triple batch and 2 1/2 for a quadruple batch. **"
---
