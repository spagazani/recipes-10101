tags: unclassified
title: "Tortilla Swirls"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "3/4 cup light cream cheese"
      - "1/2 cup low fat sour cream"
      - "1 cup shredded light cheddar"
      - "1/2 green pepper, finely chopped"
      - "8 green onions, thinly sliced"
      - "2 tsp fresh lime juice"
      - "9 6-inch flour tortillas"
      - "1/4 cup salsa"
instructions:
  - "In a small bowl, beat cream cheese and sour cream until smooth. On low speed, blend in the next 4 ingredients."
  - "Place 1/4 cup of the cheese mixture on each tortilla; roll up and place seam side down on a plate."
  - "Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate until chilled, at least 1 hour."
  - "Cut each tortilla into slices; arrange cut side up on a serving platter and top each with a 1/4 tsp salsa. Makes 12 servings."
