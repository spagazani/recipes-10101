--- 
title: "Cracker Barrel Old Country Store Biscuits"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "2 1/4 cups Bisquick Mix"
      - "2/3 cups buttermilk"
      - "1 tsp sugar"
      - "1 Tbsp Butter, melted"
instructions:
  - "Preheat oven to 450F. Mix first three ingredients together, add 1 Tbsp of the melted butter into batter. Stir ingredients until soft dough forms."
  - "Turn onto surface that has been dusted with flour. Knead 20 times, roll 1/2-inch thick, and place in an ungreased 8” square cake pan."
  - "Place biscuits next to each other, when you have placed all of them in there flatten slightly. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes."
---
