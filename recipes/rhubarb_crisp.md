--- 
title: "Rhubarb Crisp"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "2 lbs rhubarb sliced 3/4 inch thick"
      - "1 1/2 cup sugar"
      - "3/4 cup flour"
      - "1 stick butter"
      - "1 cup oatmeal"
      - "1/2 tsp cinnamon"
instructions:
  - "Preheat oven to 400F. Place rhubarb into a 9x13” baking dish; toss with 1 cup sugar and 1/4 cup flour."
  - "Cut in 1/2 flour with butter until clumps are pea sized. Add 1/2 cup sugar, oatmeal, and cinnamon, stir just till combined."
  - "Sprinkle over rhubarb. Bake until rhubarb is tender, and topping is golden, 35 minutes. Serve warm. Makes 12 servings."
---
