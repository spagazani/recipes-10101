--- 
title: "Gramma's Scalloped Potatoes"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "Sliced potatoes"
      - "3 large onions"
      - "3/4 cup butter or margarine"
      - "3 Tbsp chopped onion"
      - "3/4 cup flour"
      - "1 1/2 tsp salt"
      - "3/4 tsp dry mustard"
      - "Few grains pepper"
      - "6 cups milk"
      - "3 cups shredded cheddar cheese"
instructions:
  - "Parboil full dutch oven of potatoes. Sauté onions until tender."
  - "Melt margarine in a large saucepan, add onion and cook slowly until tender but not browned. Blend in flour, salt, mustard, and pepper."
  - "Gradually stir in milk, cooking over medium heat until thickened. Add cheese and stir until melted."
  - "Mix together potatoes, onions, and cheese sauce. Pour into a 13x9” pan, cover with foil and bake for 1 hour."
  - "Remove the foil and broil until the top of the casserole is brownish."
---
