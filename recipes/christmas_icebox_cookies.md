title: "Christmas Icebox Cookies"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 cup soft butter"
      - "3/4 cup brown sugar"
      - "1/2 tsp vanilla"
      - "1/8 tsp salt"
      - "2 1/2 cups flour"
      - "1 cup sliced almonds"
      - "1 cup red and green cherries"
instructions:
  - "Cream butter, sugar, and vanilla. Combine with flour and salt. Then add nuts and cherries and form into rolls."
  - "Wrap in wax paper and leave in ice box overnight. Slice thinly with a sharp knife and bake on a greased baking sheet at 375F for 10 minutes."
