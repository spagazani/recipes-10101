--- 
tags: brownies and bars
title: "Gramma's Date Squares"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 cup chopped dates"
      - "1 Tbsp brown sugar"
      - "1/2 cup water"
      - "1 cup flour"
      - "1/2 tsp baking soda"
      - "1/8 tsp salt"
      - "1 cup butter or margarine"
      - "1 cup light packed brown sugar"
      - "2 cups rolled oats"
instructions:
  - "Combine dates, brown sugar, and water in a saucepan. Bring to a boil and cook until dates are soft enough to be mashed with a fork, about 5 minutes. The mixture should be the consistency of jam. Set aside to cool."
  - "Preheat oven to 325F. Blend together flour, salt, and baking soda. Cut in margarine then blend in brown sugar and oats."
  - "Press half of this mixture over the bottom of a greased 8” square pan. Cover evenly with the cool date filling. Then cover with the remaining crumb mixture and pat until smooth."
  - "Bake for 35 to 40 minutes or until a light golden brown. Cool before cutting into squares."
---
