--- 
title: "Phyllo Pastry"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "9 heaping teacups flour"
      - "2 1/2 cups lukewarm water"
      - "1 tsp yeast"
      - "1 tsp salt"
      - "2 Tbsp oil"
instructions:
  - "Mix 1/2 cups lukewarm water with yeast, let soak until foamy."
  - "Combine all ingredients and knead them all together until the flour is taken up, adding water as needed. Keep kneading. Form into balls and cover, let stand for about 10 minutes."
  - "Work out one ball, like pizza and roll very thin, Fold top to centre and sprinkle with butter. Repeat with bottom, then each side to make 8 layers. Allow to stand for another 10 minutes. Roll out again, very thin, onto a large pan."
  - "Cover with your choice of Peeta fillings. Top with second piece of dough, which has been prepared as above. Sprinkle with butter and seal edges."
  - "Bake at 350F for 30 to 40 minutes until nice and brown."
  - "Note whatever you use in flour, then use half that amount in water."
---
