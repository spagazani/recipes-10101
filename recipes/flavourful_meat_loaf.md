--- 
title: "Flavourful Meat Loaf"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "2 egg whites"
      - "1/2 cup 1% milk"
      - "3 slices whole wheat bread, torn into pieces"
      - "1/4 cup finely chopped onion"
      - "1 tsp Worcestershire sauce"
      - "1/4 tsp onion powder"
      - "1/4 tsp garlic powder"
      - "1/4 tsp ground mustard"
      - "1/4 tsp rubbed sage"
      - "1/4 tsp pepper"
      - "1 lb lean ground beef"
      - "3 Tbsp ketchup"
instructions:
  - "In a large bowl, beat egg whites. Add milk and bread, let stand 5 minutes. Stir in the onion, Worcestershire sauce and seasonings."
  - "Crumble beef over mixture and mix well. Shape into a load in an 11”x7”x2” baking pan coated with non-stick cooking spray."
  - "Bake uncovered at 350F for 35 minutes, drain. Spoon ketchup over loaf. Bake 10 – 12 minutes longer or until a meat thermometer read 160."
  - "Let stand for 10 minutes before slicing."
---
