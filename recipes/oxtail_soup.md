--- 
title: "Oxtail Soup"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "Oxtails"
      - "2 large onions"
      - "1/2 pkg pot barley"
      - "1-28oz tin tomatoes"
      - "Salt (lots)"
instructions:
  - "Place oxtails in dutch oven, cover with cold water 2” from top of the pot and bring to a boil. Simmer for 1 hour."
  - "Add onions, barley, tomatoes, and salt to taste. Bring to a boil again and simmer for 3 hours."
---
