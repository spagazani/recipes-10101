tags: unclassified
title: "Homemade Turtles"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 1/3 cups chopped pecans"
      - "Parchment or waxed paper"
      - "8oz caramel candy"
      - "16oz chocolate chopped into 1-inch pieces"
instructions:
  - "Arrange pecans by heaping teaspoonfuls on your wax paper. Spread out to a single layer to form a 1 1/2” circle. Space the cluster about 3” apart."
  - "Melt the caramel in the microwave at low or 30% power for 2 minutes. Stir well. If not fully melted, microwave at 20 second intervals, stirring well after each time, until caramel is a gooey spoonable texture but not thin and runny."
  - "Using an oiled teaspoon, place a heaping spoonful of caramel into the center or each cluster of pecans."
  - "While the caramel is setting, melt the chocolate in the microwave at 30% power for 3 minutes. Stir well. Continue at 10 to 20 second intervals until it is completely melted, stirring well after each time."
  - "Once caramel is fully set, take a scant tablespoon of chocolate, and pour over caramel and pecans. Makes 32 pecans."
