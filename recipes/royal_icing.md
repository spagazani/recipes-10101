--- 
title: "Royal Icing"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 lb icing sugar"
      - "5 Tbsp meringue powder"
      - "Scant 1/2 cup water"
      - "Food colouring"
instructions:
  - "Combine all ingredients on low speed. Mix until fluffy yet dense, 7 to 8 minutes."
  - "Use icing immediately, or transfer to an airtight container. Beat well with a spatula before using."
  - "To thin royal icing for flooding, stir in additional water 1 tsp at a time."
  - "Test consistency by lifting spoon and letting icing drip back into bowl; a ribbon should remain for 5 to 7 seconds before settling back."
---
