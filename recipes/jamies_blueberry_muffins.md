--- 
title: "Jamie's Blueberry Muffins"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 1/2 cups flour"
      - "1/2 cup sugar"
      - "2 1/2 tsp baking powder"
      - "1/4 tsp salt"
      - "1 egg"
      - "3/4 cup milk"
      - "1/3 cup melted butter"
      - "1 cup blueberries, fresh or frozen"
instructions:
  - "Mix dry ingredients; add milk, egg, and butter. Stir just till moist. Then stir in blueberries."
  - "Bake at 400F for 30 minutes."
---
