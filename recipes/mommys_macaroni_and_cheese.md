tags: unclassified
title: "Mommy's Macaroni And Cheese"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 cup macaroni"
      - "1/4 cup butter or margarine"
      - "1 Tbsp chopped onion"
      - "1/4 cup flour"
      - "1/4 tsp salt"
      - "1/4 tsp dry mustard"
      - "Few grains pepper"
      - "2 cups milk"
      - "1 cup shredded cheddar cheese"
      - "2/3 cup buttered breadcrumbs"
instructions:
  - "Cook macaroni until tender in boiling salted water."
  - "Melt margarine in a large saucepan, add onion and cook slowly until tender, but not browned. Blend in flour, salt, mustard, and pepper."
  - "Gradually stir in milk, cooking over medium heat until thickened. Add cheese and stir until melted. Add macaroni and blend well."
  - "Pour into a buttered 2 qt casserole. Butter both sides of bread slices and slice them into cubes."
  - "Sprinkle over top of the casserole dish and bake at 375F for about 30 minutes or until bubbly and browned."
