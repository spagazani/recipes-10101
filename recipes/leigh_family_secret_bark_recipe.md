--- 
title: "Leigh Family Secret Bark Recipe"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 lb milk chocolate Merkin chocolate discs"
      - "1 can Pringle chips – regular are fine"
      - "1 cup peanuts"
instructions:
  - "Melt the Merkin chocolate in a bowl in the microwave as per the Bulk Barn instructions provided above the bin."
  - "Crush the Pringles into medium sized pieces and add them to the chocolate."
  - "Add the peanuts and stir until coated."
  - "Spread the mixture onto wax paper lined cookie sheets and let cool. Break into pieces and wrap for later or enjoy!"
---
