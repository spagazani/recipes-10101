--- 
title: "Harvey Wallbanger Cake"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 2-layer orange or lemon cake mix"
      - "1 4 oz. Package of vanilla pudding"
      - "4 eggs"
      - "1/2 cup cooking oil"
      - "1/2 cup orange juice"
      - "1/2 cup Liquor Galliano"
      - "2 Tbsp Vodka"
      - "1 cup icing sugar"
      - "1 Tbsp orange juice"
      - "1 Tbsp Liquor Galliano"
      - "1 tsp Vodka"
instructions:
  - "In a large mixing bowl, combine cake mix and pudding mix. Add eggs, oil, orange juice and liqueurs."
  - "Beat on low speed for one to two minutes, beat on medium speed for 5 minutes, scraping bowl frequently."
  - "Pour into greased, floured and fluted 10” bundt pan. Bake at 350F for 45 minutes. Cool in pan for 10 minutes."
  - "Combine the rest of the ingredients and mix well. Remove cake to rack and pour on glaze while cake is still warm."
---
