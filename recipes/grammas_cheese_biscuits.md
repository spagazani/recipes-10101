--- 
title: "Gramma's Cheese Biscuits"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "2 cups flour"
      - "4 tsp baking powder"
      - "1 tsp salt"
      - "3/4 cup shredded cheddar"
      - "1/3 cup shortening"
      - "3/4 cup + 2 Tbsp milk"
instructions:
  - "Blend together flour, baking powder, salt, cheese. Cut in finely, shortening until crumbly. Add milk and stir with fork to make soft dough."
  - "Turn dough onto a lightly floured surface and knead gently 8 to 10 times. Roll or pat to desired thickness. Cut out with a floured cutter."
  - "Place biscuits on an ungreased baking sheet, close together for soft sided biscuits or about 1” apart for crusty biscuits."
  - "Bake in preheated 450F oven for 12 to 15 minutes. Make 18 to 20, 1 3/4” biscuits."
---
