title: "Sweet Soy Sauce Pork Squares"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "2lb pork loin"
      - "2 Tbsp vegetable oil"
      - "1 Tbsp garlic and ginger past"
      - "1 Tbsp olive oil"
      - "1 Tbsp sesame oil"
      - "1/2 cup soy sauce"
      - "4 Tbsp sugar"
      - "1 1/2 cups water"
      - "1 Tbsp chili garlic sauce"
instructions:
  - "Cut pork into 1-inch pieces and sauté in a pan with the vegetable oil for about 3 minutes over medium-high heat until pork is no longer pink and starts to brown."
  - "Mix the rest of the ingredients and pour over the pork. Bring to a boil and then turn heat down to low."
  - "Simmer for about 30 minutes uncovered, stirring occasionally until there is only about 3 Tbsp of sauce left."
