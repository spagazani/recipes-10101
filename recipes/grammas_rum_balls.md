title: "Gramma's Rum Balls"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1/2 cup butter"
      - "3/4 cup chocolate chips"
      - "2 eggs lightly beaten"
      - "1/2 cup icing sugar"
      - "2 Tbsp dark rum"
      - "1 3/4 cups vanilla wafer crumbs"
      - "Chocolate hail"
      - "Coconut"
instructions:
  - "Melt first 4 ingredients in a double boiler over hot water. Stir until smooth and thick. Remove from heat and add rum and wafer crumbs."
  - "Stir in and place in fridge for at least 4 hours."
  - "Roll into balls and roll in chocolate or coconut."
