--- 
tags: beef
title: "Michelle's Herbed Rump Roast"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 3 lb roast beef"
      - "2 Tbsp oil"
      - "1 cup onion, chopped"
      - "1 tsp beef bouillon granules"
      - "1 tsp marjoram"
      - "1 tsp thyme"
      - "1 bay leaf"
      - "1 clove garlic, halved"
      - "1/4 cup flour"
      - "1 Tbsp parsley"
instructions:
  - "Place oil in bottom of roasting pan. Place roast beef on top."
  - "Combine onion, bouillon, marjoram, thyme, bay leaf, garlic, 1/2 cup water and 1/4 tsp pepper. Add to roasting pan."
  - "Bake covered in a 350F oven for 2 to 3 hours or until tender. Remove meat from pan, discard bay leaf."
  - "For gravy, measure pan juices, if necessary, add potato water to equal 1 3/4 cups."
  - "Combine flour, parsley, and 1/2 cup water. Stir into juices; return to pan."
  - "Cook and stir until thickened and bubbly, and then for 1 minute more. Season to taste."
