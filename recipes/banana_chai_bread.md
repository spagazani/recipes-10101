--- 
title: "Banana Chai Bread"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 3/4 cups flour"
      - "1 Tbsp baking powder"
      - "1/2 tsp salt"
      - "3/4 cup sugar"
      - "1/2 cup low fat cream cheese"
      - "2 eggs"
      - "3/4 cup mashed banana"
      - "1/4 cup brewed chai tea"
instructions:
  - "Mix together flour, baking powder and salt. In a separate bowl mix sugar, cream cheese, and eggs until light and fluffy."
  - "Mix mashed bananas and chai into cream cheese mixture. Add flour mixture and mix until smooth."
  - "Pour mixture into a greased 9x5” loaf pan. Bake in a preheated 350F oven for 60 minutes. Cool on rack."
  - "Remove from pan after 10 minutes. Makes 12 servings."
---
