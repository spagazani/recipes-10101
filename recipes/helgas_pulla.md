--- 
title: "Helga's Pulla"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "14 cardamom seeds, crushed"
      - "1 1/2 cups sugar"
      - "2 1/2 cups milk, room temperature"
      - "1/2 tsp salt"
      - "4 eggs, room temperature"
      - "1 cup butter, room temperature"
      - "2 cubes fresh yeast"
      - "Flour"
instructions:
  - "Finely crush cardamom after taking out of pods. In a large bowl, combine sugar, milk, salt, eggs, butter, and yeast."
  - "Add cardamom and enough flour until the dough doesn't stick to your hand anymore. Put butter on top of dough so that it doesn't harden."
  - "While rising on top of a warm stove, cover with a damp tea towel. Let it rise for about two hours or until doubled in bulk."
  - "Punch it down and cut it into 5 chunks and divide each by 3. Braid each set of three and place on a greased baking sheet."
  - "Brush with an egg/sugar mixture and bake for 30 minutes at 375F. Makes about 5 pullas."
---
