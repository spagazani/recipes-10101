--- 
title: "Kathy Malone's Tuxedo Cookie Bars"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 1/2 cups flour"
      - "1 tsp baking soda"
      - "1/2 cup salted butter"
      - "1 1/2 cups sugar"
      - "3 cups semisweet chocolate chips"
      - "1 Tbsp vanilla"
      - "4 Tbsp hot water"
      - "4 large eggs"
      - "1 cup white chocolate chips"
instructions:
  - "Preheat oven to 325F. Grease a 13x9” pan. In a medium bowl combine flour and soda with a whisk. Set aside."
  - "Melt butter and 2 cups of the semisweet chips over low heat stirring constantly until smooth. In a large bowl, beat chocolate mixture and sugar until smooth, about 5 minutes."
  - "Continue to beat, add vanilla, then water a tablespoon at a time. Scrape down sides of bowl, then add eggs one at a time. Mix well."
  - "Gradually add flour mixture, blending at low speed. Then add the remaining semisweet chips and the white chocolate chips. Blend until equally distributed throughout the batter."
  - "Spread batter into prepared baking pan. Bake 35 to 40 minutes or until tester comes out slightly moist. Cool to room temperature."
  - "Cover well and refrigerate until cold. Cut into bars and serve."
---
