--- 
title: "Speedy Chicken Parmesan"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "4 4oz chicken breasts"
      - "1 large egg, slightly beaten"
      - "1/2 cup Italian breadcrumbs"
      - "1 1/2 Tbsp margarine"
      - "Cooking spray"
      - "1 3/4 cups fire roasted tomato and garlic pasta sauce"
      - "1/2 cup mozzarella cheese"
      - "1 Tbsp Parmesan cheese"
      - "1/4 cup chopped parsley"
      - "3 cup hot cooked spaghetti"
instructions:
  - "Place chicken between 2 sheets of plastic wrap; flatten to 1/4” thickness using a meat mallet. Dip chicken in egg, and dredge in breadcrumbs."
  - "Melt butter in a large skillet coated with cooking spray over medium high heat. Add chicken; cook 4 minutes on each side or until browned."
  - "Spoon pasta sauce over chicken; cover, reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes. Sprinkle with cheeses and parsley; cover and simmer an additional 5 minutes or until cheeses melt."
  - "Spoon 3/4 cup pasta onto each of 4 plates; top each with a chicken breast and divide sauce evenly over each serving."
---
