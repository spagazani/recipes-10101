--- 
title: "Gramma's Fightin' Food Short Rib Sauce"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 cup ketchup"
      - "1 cup water"
      - "1 Tbsp cider vinegar"
      - "1 Tbsp Worcestershire"
      - "1 Tbsp dry mustard"
      - "1 tsp salt"
      - "1/4 tsp pepper"
      - "1 bay leaf"
      - "2 onions"
instructions:
  - "Bring ingredients to a boil and simmer to let sauce thicken. Makes enough sauce for 3/4 lbs beef short ribs."
---
