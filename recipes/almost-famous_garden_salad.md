--- 
title: "Almost-Famous Garden Salad"
ingredients:
  - section: "Dressing"
    items:
      - "1/4 cup extra olive oil"
      - "2 Tbsp white wine vinegar"
      - "3 Tbsp Miracle Whip"
      - "1 Tbsp lemon juice"
      - "2 Tbsp grated Parmesan cheese"
      - "1/4 tsp garlic salt"
      - "1/2 tsp dried Italian seasoning"
  - section: "Salad"
    items:
      - "1 10-ounce bag American salad blend"
      - "1/4 red onion, thinly sliced"
      - "4 small, pickled peppers (pepperoncini)"
      - "1 small tomato, quartered"
      - "2 Tbsp sliced black olives"
      - "1/2 cup large croutons"
      - "1 Tbsp grated Parmesan cheese"
instructions:
  - "Combine all the dressing ingredients and 1-2 Tbsp of water in a blender or food processor until smooth."
  - "Place the salad blend in a large bowl and top with remaining ingredients. Drizzle with the dressing and toss."
---
