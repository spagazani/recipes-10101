--- 
title: "Hot Tortilla Bacon Swirls"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 tsp olive oil"
      - "1/2 small red onion, sliced"
      - "1-10oz bag fresh spinach, trimmed"
      - "1 1/2 cups cream cheese, softened"
      - "3 Tbsp minced fresh coriander"
      - "1 Tbsp minced jalapeno pepper"
      - "1/2 tsp pepper"
      - "1/4 tsp salt"
      - "10 small flour tortillas"
      - "1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese"
      - "8 slices cooked bacon, crumbled"
instructions:
  - "In a skillet, heat oil over medium heat and fry onion just until softened and move to a bowl."
  - "In a saucepan, cook rinsed spinach over medium-high heat, stirring occasionally, until wilted."
  - "Put spinach in a sieve, let cool, then press out moisture and chop. Add to bowl with onion."
  - "In a separate bowl, beat together cream cheese, coriander, jalapeno, pepper, and salt until smooth."
  - "Divide among tortillas, spreading evenly. Top with spinach mixture, leaving 2 inches cream cheese uncovered on one side."
  - "Sprinkle with cheese and bacon."
  - "Roll up toward exposed cream cheese, press to seal."
  - "Refrigerate until firm (~1 hour). Cut into slices and place on sheet lined with foil."
  - "Broil, 8 inches from heat until golden and cheese is melted. Alternatively, they can be sliced and put into a container then frozen for up to 1 month."
