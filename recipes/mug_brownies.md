--- 
title: "Mug Brownies"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "2 Tbsp butter, melted"
      - "2 Tbsp water"
      - "1/4 tsp vanilla"
      - "Dash of salt"
      - "4 Tbsp white sugar"
      - "2 Tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder"
      - "4 Tbsp of all purpose flour"
instructions:
  - "In a 12oz coffee mug, add water, butter, vanilla, and salt. Whisk well."
  - "Add cocoa powder. Whisk. Add sugar. Whisk. Add flour. Whisk."
  - "Cook in microwave for 1 1/2 - 2 minutes."
---
