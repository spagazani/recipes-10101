tags: unclassified
title: "Italian Relish"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 Tbsp margarine"
      - "1 lb ground beef"
      - "1/2 cup chopped onions"
      - "1/3 cup sliced green peppers"
      - "2 cups canned tomatoes, drained"
      - "1 10oz can tomato soup"
      - "1 tsp salt"
      - "1/4 tsp oregano"
      - "1/4 tsp garlic powder"
      - "Few grains cayenne"
      - "Pasta"
      - "3/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese"
instructions:
  - "Melt margarine in a large pot. Add beef, onions, and green peppers and brown lightly."
  - "Add tomatoes, soup, and spices and enough pasta to thicken up the sauce. Be careful not to add too much as it will cause the sauce to stick and burn to the bottom of the pot. Stir to combine."
  - "Simmer very slowly for 20 minutes. Just before serving stir in cheddar cheese. Makes 6 servings."
