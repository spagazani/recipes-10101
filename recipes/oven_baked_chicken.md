--- 
title: "Oven Baked Chicken"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "4 chicken breasts"
      - "2 tsp olive oil"
      - "1 1/2 Tbsp brown sugar"
      - "1 tsp paprika"
      - "1 tsp dried oregano or thyme"
      - "1/4 tsp garlic powder"
      - "1/2 tsp each salt and pepper"
instructions:
  - "Preheat oven to 425F degrees."
  - "Pound chicken to 1.5 cm at the thickest part."
  - "Mix seasoning."
  - "Line tray with foil and parchment paper. Place chicken upside down on tray. Drizzle with about 1 tsp oil. Rub all over with fingers and sprinkle with seasoning."
  - "Flip chicken and repeat with other side, covering as much as you can with the seasoning mix."
  - "Bake for 18 minutes or until surface is golden."
  - "Remove from oven and transfer to serving plate, wait 3-5 minutes before serving."
---
