--- 
title: "Whipped Cream"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 cup whip cream"
      - "3 Tbsp sugar"
      - "Few drops vanilla or almond extract"
instructions:
  - "Using an electric mixer and beat chilled whip cream until it just moulds."
  - "To sweeten, add sugar once the cream thickens and beat until the cream just hold its shape."
  - "Add vanilla or almond extract to taste."
---
