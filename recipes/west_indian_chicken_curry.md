--- 
tags: chicken
title: "West Indian Chicken Curry"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "3 Tbsp vegetable oil"
      - "3 lbs of chicken, cut into pieces"
      - "1 large onion, diced"
      - "6 cloves garlic, minced"
      - "4 large potatoes – peeled and cubed"
      - "2 Tbsp salt"
      - "1/4 cup Beta Curry Powder – from West Indian Store"
instructions:
  - "Heat the oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Cook the chicken, onions, and garlic until the chicken has browned; about 5 minutes. Stir in potatoes, salt, and curry powder; add enough water to cover the chicken halfway. Cover, and simmer until tender, about 30 to 40 minutes."
  - "Serve with rice or with bake."
