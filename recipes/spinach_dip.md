--- 
title: "Spinach Dip"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 cup mayonnaise"
      - "1 whole container of sour cream"
      - "1 pkg of dry vegetable soup mix"
      - "1 can of water chestnuts diced very small"
      - "1 pkg of frozen chopped spinach"
      - "1 round pumpernickel or sour dough bread"
instructions:
  - "Thaw spinach and squeeze liquid out. Dice water chestnuts very small. Mix all ingredients together, cover and refrigerate until cold."
  - "Carve out the center of the loaf of the bread and chop into bite size squares."
  - "Spoon dip into the bread shell and arrange bread pieces around and on top of the dip. Serve."
---
