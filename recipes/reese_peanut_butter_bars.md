--- 
tags: brownies and bars
title: "Reese Peanut Butter Bars"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 cup salted butter, melted"
      - "2 cups graham cracker crumbs"
      - "1/4 cup brown sugar"
      - "1 3/4 cup powdered sugar"
      - "1 cup peanut butter"
      - "1/2 tsp vanilla"
      - "1 (11oz) bag milk chocolate chips"
instructions:
  - "Combine all ingredients, except chocolate chips, in a medium sized bowl. Stir until the mixture is smooth and creamy."
  - "Pour peanut butter mixture into a 9x13 pan."
  - "Melt chocolate chips in the microwave for 1-2 minutes. Stir chocolate and pour over peanut butter mixture. Spread chocolate with a spatula."
  - "Refrigerate bars for one hour. Cut while bars are cool."
