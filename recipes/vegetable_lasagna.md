tags: unclassified
title: "Vegetable Lasagna"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 1/2 cups light chunky mushroom and garlic pasta sauce"
      - "1 14oz can stewed tomatoes, chopped, and undrained"
      - "2 cups fat free cottage cheese"
      - "1/2 cup Parmesan cheese"
      - "1/4 tsp pepper"
      - "Cooking spray"
      - "9 uncooked lasagna noodles"
      - "3 cups shredded zucchini"
      - "6 1oz slices provolone cheese, each cut into 6 strips"
instructions:
  - "Preheat oven to 350F. Combine pasta sauce and tomatoes; stir well and set aside."
  - "Combine cottage cheese, Parmesan, and pepper; stir well and set aside."
  - "Spread 1/3 tomato mixture into the bottom of a 13x9” baking dish coated with cooking spray."
  - "Place 3 uncooked lasagna noodles over tomato mixture; top with 1/3 zucchini. Spread 1/3 cottage cheese mixture evenly over zucchini. Top with 12 provolone cheese strips."
  - "Repeat layers twice with remaining ingredients."
  - "Cover and bake at 350F for 45 minutes. Uncover, and bake an additional 15 minutes or until thoroughly heated. Let stand 15 minutes before serving."
