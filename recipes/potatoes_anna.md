--- 
title: "Potatoes Anna"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 tsp kosher or sea salt"
      - "1/2 tsp pepper"
      - "2 1/2 Tbsp butter"
      - "3 lbs peeled baking potatoes, cut into 1/8-inch slices"
      - "1 Tbsp butter, melted and divided"
      - "1 Tbsp chopped fresh parsley"
instructions:
  - "Preheat oven to 450F. Combine salt and pepper in a small bowl. Melt 2 1/2 Tbsp and place in a casserole dish."
  - "Arrange a single layer of potato slices, slightly overlapping in a circular pattern; sprinkle with 1/4 tsp salt mixture. Drizzle 1/2 tsp melted butter over potatoes. Repeat layers 5 times, ending with butter. Press firmly to pack."
  - "Cover and bake at 450F for 20 minutes. Uncover and bake an additional 25 minutes or until potatoes are golden. Sprinkle with parsley if desired."
---
