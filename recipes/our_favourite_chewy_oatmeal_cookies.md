--- 
title: "Our Favourite Chewy Oatmeal Cookies"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1/2 cup shortening"
      - "1/2 cup brown sugar"
      - "1/2 cup sugar"
      - "1 beaten egg"
      - "1 Tbsp water"
      - "1/2 tsp vanilla"
      - "3/4 cup flour"
      - "1/2 tsp baking soda"
      - "1/2 tsp salt"
      - "1 1/2 cups Quaker oats"
      - "1 1/2 cups raisins"
instructions:
  - "Cream shortening and sugars. Stir in egg, water, and vanilla. Add remaining ingredients."
  - "Teaspoon on a greased baking sheet. Bake 10-15 minutes in a 375F oven."
---
