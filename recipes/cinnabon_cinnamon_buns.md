tags: unclassified
title: "Cinnabon Cinnamon Buns"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "2 pkg dry active yeast"
      - "1 cup warm water (105-115 degrees)"
      - "2/3 cup plus 1 tsp sugar divided"
      - "1 cup warmed milk"
      - "2/3 cup butter"
      - "2 tsp salt"
      - "2 eggs, slightly beaten"
      - "7 cups flour, or more if needed"
      - "Filling"
      - "1 cup melted butter, divided"
      - "1 3/4 cups sugar, divided"
      - "3 Tbsp cinnamon"
      - "1 1/2 cups walnuts, optional"
      - "1 1/2 cups raisins, optional"
      - "Glaze"
      - "2/3 cup melted butter"
      - "4 cups icing sugar"
      - "2 tsp vanilla"
      - "4 Tbsp hot water"
instructions:
  - "Combine yeast, warm water, and 1 tsp sugar, stir, and set aside. Mix milk and 2/3 cup sugar, melted butter, salt and eggs; stir well and add yeast."
  - "Add half the flour and beat until smooth. Stir in enough of the flour until dough is slightly stiff and sticky."
  - "Turn out onto well floured board and knead for 5 to 10 minutes. Place in buttered glass or plastic bowl, cover and let rise in warm place free from drafts, until doubled in bulk. About 1 to 1/2 hours."
  - "When doubled, punch down dough and let rest 5 minutes. Roll out into floured surface into a 15x20” rectangle. Spread dough with 1/2 cup melted butter. Mix 1 1/2 cups sugar and cinnamon, sprinkle over buttered dough. Sprinkle with walnuts and raisins if desired."
  - "Roll up jellyroll style and pinch the edge together to seal. Cut into 12 slices. Coat bottom of a 13x9” baking pan and 8” square pan with remaining 1/2 cup melted butter, sprinkle with remaining 1/2 cup sugar."
  - "Place cinnamon roll slices close together in pans. Let rise in warm place until dough is doubled in bulk, about 45 minutes. Preheat oven to 350F. Bake 25 to 30 minutes or until rolls are nicely browned. Cool rolls slightly."
  - "In a medium bowl, mix melted butter, powdered sugar, and vanilla; add hot water 1 Tbsp at a time until glaze reaches desired spreading consistency. Spread over slightly cooled rolls."
