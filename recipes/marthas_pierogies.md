tags: unclassified
title: "Martha's Pierogies"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 large egg"
      - "2 Tbsp sour cream"
      - "1 cup milk"
      - "1 cup water"
      - "4 1/2 to 5 cups flour plus more for dusting"
      - "5 lbs baking potatoes, peeled and quartered"
      - "12 Tbsp unsalted butter, melted"
      - "2oz cheddar cheese, grated"
      - "4 oz cream cheese"
      - "Salt and pepper"
instructions:
  - "Whisk egg lightly, add sour cream and whisk until smooth. Add milk and water, and whisk until combined. Slowly add about 3 cups of flour and stir with a wooden spoon until combined."
  - "Turn out onto a well floured surface. Work in about a cup of flour as you knead. Continue kneading for 8 to 10 minutes working in another 1/2 cup flour. The dough should be elastic and not sticky."
  - "Place dough in lightly floured bowl and cover. Let rest."
  - "Cover potatoes in a large pot with cold water, add salt. Bring to a boil on high heat. Cook until fork tender."
  - "Drain and mash, add 4 Tbsp melted butter and the cheeses and mash until combined. Season with salt and pepper or onion or garlic powder."
  - "Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil over high heat."
  - "On a floured surface, roll dough to 1/8” thick. Using a glass about 2 1/2” in diameter, cut out as many circles as possible."
  - "Form filling into 1 1/2” balls and place into the centre of each circle. Fold over dough and pinch the edges closed."
  - "Place pierogi into boiling water in batches. They will sink to the bottom. Once they float, let them cook for about a minute more. Roll in butter and freeze for further use."
