--- 
title: "King Arthur's Pumpkin Pie"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1/2 cup white sugar"
      - "1/2 cup brown sugar, packed"
      - "1 tbsp all purpose flour"
      - "1/2 tsp salt"
      - "1 tsp ginger"
      - "1 tsp cinnamon"
      - "1/2 tsp nutmeg"
      - "1/8 tsp cloves"
      - "1/8 tsp black pepper"
      - "3 eggs, beaten"
      - "2 cups canned pumpkin"
      - "1 1/4 cups light cream or evaporated milk"
      - "Pie crust - use your favourite"
instructions:
  - "In a large bowl, beat together eggs, pumpkin, and cream. Whisk into dry ingredients. For best flavour, cover and refrigerate overnight before baking."
  - "Prepare pie crust in a 9” pie plate and preheat oven to 400F. Once hot, place the pie on a baking sheet to catch any drips. Pour filling into unbaked shell."
  - "Bake for 45 to 50 minutes, until the filling is set 2” from the edge. The centre should still be wobbly. Remove pie from oven and cool on rack."
---
