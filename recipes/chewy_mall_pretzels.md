--- 
tags: quick breads
title: "Chewy Mall Pretzels"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 1/2 cup warm water"
      - "1 1/8 tsp dry yeast"
      - "2 Tbsp brown sugar"
      - "1 1/8 tsp salt"
      - "1 cup bread flour"
      - "3 cups flour"
      - "2 cups warm water"
      - "2 Tbsp baking soda"
      - "To taste, coarse salt"
      - "2 to 4 Tbsp butter, melted"
instructions:
  - "Sprinkle yeast on lukewarm water in mixing bowl; stir to dissolve. Add sugar, salt, and stir to dissolve; add flour and knead dough until smooth and elastic. Let rise at least 1/2 hour."
  - "While dough is rising, prepare a baking soda water bath with 2 cups warm water and 2 tbsp baking soda. Be certain to stir often."
  - "After dough has risen, pinch off bits of dough and roll into a long rope about 1/2 inch or less thick and shape."
  - "Dip pretzels in soda solution and place on a greased baking sheet. Allow pretzels to rise again."
  - "Bake in an oven at 450F for about 10 minutes or golden. Brush with melted butter and enjoy."
  - "After you brush with butter try sprinkling with coarse salt."
