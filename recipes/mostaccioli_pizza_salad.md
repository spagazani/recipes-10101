--- 
title: "Mostaccioli Pizza Salad"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "3 Tbsp white vinegar"
      - "2 Tbsp water"
      - "1 Tbsp olive oil"
      - "3/4 tsp oregano"
      - "1/4 tsp salt"
      - "1/4 tsp pepper"
      - "2 clove garlic, crushed"
      - "2 3/4 cup cooked tubular pasta"
      - "1 cup pre-sliced mushrooms"
      - "3/4 cup cherry tomatoes, halved"
      - "1/2 cup chopped green pepper"
      - "1/2 cup shredded mozzarella"
instructions:
  - "Combine first 7 ingredients in a medium bowl, stirring with a whisk until blended."
  - "Add pasta and next 4 ingredients; toss gently to coat. Cover and chill."
---
