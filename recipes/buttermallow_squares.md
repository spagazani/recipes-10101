--- 
tags: brownies and bars
title: "Buttermallow Squares"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 cup butterscotch chips"
      - "1/3 cup peanut butter"
      - "4 Tbsp butter or margarine"
      - "2 cups mini marshmallows"
      - "3 cups Rice Krispies cereal"
instructions:
  - "In a saucepan over low heat, combine butterscotch chips, peanut butter, and butter. Stir constantly until melted and well blended. Remove from heat."
  - "Add marshmallows and cereal. Stir until well coated."
  - "Press mixture firmly into lightly greased 9” square pan. Refrigerate until firm."
  - "Cut into squares. Makes about 16 2” squares."
---
