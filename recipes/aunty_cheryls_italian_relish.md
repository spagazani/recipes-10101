--- 
tags: mains
title: "Aunty Cheryl's Italian Relish"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1/2 cups macaroni, uncooked"
      - "1/2 cup chopped onions"
      - "1 lb ground chuck"
      - "1 can tomatoes"
      - "1/2 cup corn or peas, optional"
      - "1/3 cup grated cheese"
      - "1/2 cup green pepper"
      - "Butter breadcrumbs"
instructions:
  - "Cook macaroni in boiling water and then drain."
  - "Cook the ground chuck and chopped onions together for 10 minutes stirring constantly."
  - "Combine all ingredients except crumbs, season with salt and pepper and put into a casserole. Cover with breadcrumbs and bake for 20 minutes in oven at 350F."
