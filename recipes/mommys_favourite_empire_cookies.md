--- 
title: "Mommy's Favourite Empire Cookies"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1/2 cup butter"
      - "1/2 cup sugar"
      - "1 egg"
      - "1 tsp vanilla"
      - "2 cups flour"
      - "1 tsp baking powder"
      - "Raspberry Jam"
      - "1 cup sifted icing sugar"
      - "1 Tbsp hot water"
      - "1/4 tsp almond extract"
      - "Cherries halved"
instructions:
  - "Preheat oven to 350F. Cream butter and sugar. Beat in egg and vanilla. Mix flour and baking powder."
  - "Add gradually to butter mixture. Roll dough to a 1/8” thickness. Cut into 2” rounds and bake 10 minutes."
  - "Cool and spread jam on top of half of the cookies. Cover with remaining cookies."
  - "Stir icing sugar, water, and extract to make a thin icing. Ice tops of cookies and garnish with a half of a cherry. Makes 1 1/2 dozen."
---
