--- 
tags: brownies and bars
title: "Rocky Road Candy"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 350g pkg semisweet chocolate chips"
      - "1 300ml can Eagle Brand milk"
      - "2 Tbsp margarine or butter"
      - "2 cups dry roasted peanuts"
      - "1 250g pkg miniature marshmallows"
instructions:
  - "In heavy saucepan, over low heat, melt chocolate chips with Eagle Brand and margarine: remove from heat."
  - "In large bowl, combine nuts and marshmallows. Then, fold into chocolate mixture."
  - "Spread in wax paper lined 13x9” pan. Chill 2 hours or until firm."
  - "Remove from pan, peel off wax paper, and cut into squares. Cover and store. Makes about 40 squares."
---
