--- 
title: "Chicken Breast Dijon"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1/3 cup breadcrumbs"
      - "1 Tbsp Parmesan cheese"
      - "1 tsp oregano"
      - "1/2 tsp thyme"
      - "1/4 tsp salt"
      - "1/4 tsp pepper"
      - "2 Tbsp Dijon mustard"
      - "4 4oz chicken breasts"
      - "1 tsp olive oil"
      - "1 tsp margarine"
instructions:
  - "Combine first 6 ingredients in a shallow, stir well. Brush mustard over both sides of chicken; dredge in breadcrumb mixture."
  - "Heat oil and margarine in a large skillet over medium high heat. Add chicken and cook 6 minutes each side or until done."
---
