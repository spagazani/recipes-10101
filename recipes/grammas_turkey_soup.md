--- 
title: "Gramma's Turkey Soup"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "Turkey carcass"
      - "6 cups cold water"
      - "1 cup celery leaves"
      - "1 Tbsp parsley"
      - "2 tsp salt"
      - "3 peppercorns"
      - "1 bay leaf"
      - "1 tsp poultry seasoning"
      - "1 large onion, sliced"
      - "1 large carrot, sliced"
      - "Any leftover dice turkey"
      - "Rice or pasta"
instructions:
  - "Break apart the turkey carcass and place in a large pot. Add water, celery leaves, parsley, and seasonings. Bring to a boil, cover, and simmer for 3 hours."
  - "Strain bones out with a colander. Measure the broth to 6 cups, adding water or vegetable broth to make up the difference."
  - "Add the diced turkey, onion, and carrots. Bring to a boil, until the vegetables are tender. Add rice or pasta, being careful not to add too much or all of your broth will be gone."
---
