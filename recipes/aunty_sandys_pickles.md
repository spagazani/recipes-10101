--- 
title: "Aunty Sandy's Pickles"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "4 lb small cucumbers"
      - "Cloves fresh garlic"
      - "Sprigs of fresh dill"
      - "1/2 cup coarse salt"
      - "3 cups white vinegar"
      - "3 cups water"
instructions:
  - "Scrub cucumbers thoroughly under cold running water. Cover cucumbers in ice cold water overnight."
  - "Drop 1/2 clove garlic and sprig of dill into hot sterilized jars. Pack in cucumber. Place more dill on top."
  - "Bring salt, vinegar, and water to a boil, stirring until salt is dissolved. Pour over cucumbers leaving 1/8” head space. Seal immediately."
  - "Store 4 to 6 weeks. Use more garlic if preferred."
---
