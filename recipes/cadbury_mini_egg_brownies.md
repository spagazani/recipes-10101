--- 
title: "Cadbury Mini Egg Brownies"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 cup butter"
      - "3 ounces milk chocolate, chopped"
      - "1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder"
      - "1 1/4 cups granulated sugar"
      - "3 eggs"
      - "1 Tbsp"
      - "1/4 cup whole milk plain Greek yogurt"
      - "1/2 cup all-purpose flour"
      - "1 tsp salt"
      - "1 1/2 cups chopped Cadbury Mini Eggs, divided"
instructions:
  - "Preheat oven to 350F degrees. Line an 8x8 inch baking pan with parchment paper."
  - "In a medium saucepan over low heat, melt chocolate and butter together. Remove from heat and add cocoa powder. Stir until smooth. Set aside."
  - "In a medium sized mixing bowl, whisk together sugar, eggs, and vanilla until combined. Next add the vanilla and salt. Add the chocolate mixture to the egg mixture and stir to combine. Stir in flour. Fold in Greek yogurt and 3/4 cup mini eggs."
  - "Pour batter into pan and spread evenly. Sprinkle remaining mini eggs on top of batter."
  - "Bake for 30-40 minutes. Let cool."
---
