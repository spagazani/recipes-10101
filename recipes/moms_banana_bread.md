--- 
title: "Mom's Banana Bread"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 3/4 cups flour"
      - "2 tsp baking powder"
      - "1/4 tsp baking soda"
      - "1/2 tsp salt"
      - "1/3 cup shortening"
      - "2/3 cup sugar"
      - "2 well beaten eggs"
      - "1 cup well mashed bananas"
instructions:
  - "Preheat oven to 350F. Blend together flour, baking powder, soda, and salt."
  - "In a large bowl cream shortening, gradually add in sugar. Add eggs and combine well. Add dry ingredients alternately with mashed bananas."
  - "Beat until combined after each addition. Pour into greased 9x5” loaf pans."
  - "Let stand for 20 minutes. Bake for 50 to 55 minutes."
---
