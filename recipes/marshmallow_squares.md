--- 
tags: brownies and bars
title: "Marshmallow Squares"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 Tbsp butter"
      - "1 cup peanut butter"
      - "8oz butterscotch chips"
      - "3 cups coloured mini marshmallows"
instructions:
  - "Barely melt first 3 ingredients over low heat."
  - "Remove from heat and stir until completely melted. Stir in marshmallows."
  - "Pour into buttered oblong pan and refrigerate until set."
---
