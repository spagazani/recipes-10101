--- 
title: "Raspberry Almond Lattice Bars"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 pkg shortbread mix"
      - "2 tsp almond extract"
      - "1 cup raspberry jam"
instructions:
  - "Prepare shortbread mix according to package directions adding almond extract to the dough. Reserve 1 cup of dough."
  - "Press remaining dough into a 9” square pan, covering bottom and partly up sides. Spread evenly with jam."
  - "Roll reserved dough into pencil like strips and place diagonally across jam to form a lattice."
  - "Bake at 325F for 35 to 45 minutes or until golden brown. Cool and cut into bars. Makes about 30 bars."
---
