--- 
title: "Sinful Hot Chocolate"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1/4 cup cocoa powder"
      - "4 cups milk"
      - "1 cup semisweet chocolate chips"
      - "1 Tbsp vanilla"
      - "Salt"
instructions:
  - "Put cocoa in a saucepan. Stir in milk a bit at a time until cocoa liquefies; add remainder. Add chips; melt over medium heat, stirring occasionally."
  - "Bring to a boil, stirring occasionally. Remove from heat, stir in vanilla and a pinch of salt."
  - "Ladle into mugs; top with whip cream if desired."
---
