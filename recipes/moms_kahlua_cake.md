title: "Mom's Kahlua Cake"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "2 oz Kahlua"
      - "4 oz milk"
      - "2 pkg chocolate chip cookies or chocolate wafer cookies"
      - "1 can crushed pineapple"
      - "1 large container Cool Whip"
      - "1 pkg lemon Jell-O"
      - "Chocolate"
      - "Walnuts"
      - "Coconut"
instructions:
  - "Dip cookies in Kahlua and milk mixture. Place 1 layer in 13x12” pan."
  - "Mix Cool Whip and Jell-O as per Aunty Joan's cherry cheesecake recipe for more firmness. Then alternate with layers of Cool Whip, pineapple, cookies, cool whip, pineapple, etc."
  - "Top with coconut, walnuts, and shaved chocolate. Chill complete dish until serving."
