--- 
title: "Martha Stewart Carrot Cake"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "6 cups all-purpose flour, plus more for the pans"
      - "2 lbs carrots, peeled and finely grated"
      - "6 large eggs, room temperature"
      - "2/3 cup non-fat buttermilk, room temperature"
      - "4 cups sugar"
      - "3 cups vegetable oil"
      - "2 vanilla beans, halved lengthwise, seeds scraped and reserved"
      - "1 cup golden raisins"
      - "4 tsp baking powder"
      - "2 tsp baking soda"
      - "2 tsp salt"
      - "2 tsp ground cinnamon"
      - "2 tsp ground ginger"
      - "1/4 tsp ground cloves"
instructions:
  - "Preheat the oven to 325F degrees. Coat the cake pans with cooking spray, line with parchment, dust with flour and tap out any access. Set aside."
  - "Whisk together the carrots, eggs, buttermilk, sugar, oil, vanilla seeds, and raisins in a large bowl."
  - "In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, ginger, and cloves."
  - "Stir the flour mixture into the carrot mixture until completely combined. Transfer the batter to the prepared pans."
  - "Bake until a cake tester inserted in the centers come out clean. Transfer to wire racks; let cool in the pans for 40 minutes. Unmold onto racks. Remove the parchment, and cool completely."
---
