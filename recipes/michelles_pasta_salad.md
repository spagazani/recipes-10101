--- 
title: "Michelle's Pasta Salad"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "Rotini pasta"
      - "Diced tomato"
      - "Cubed cheddar"
      - "Green onions, chopped"
      - "Green pepper, chopped"
      - "Frozen peas"
      - "3 Tbsp lemon juice"
      - "3 Tbsp water"
      - "2 garlic cloves, minced"
      - "2 tsp Dijon mustard"
      - "1/4 cup oil"
      - "Salt and pepper"
instructions:
  - "Cook and drain amount of pasta you wish to serve. Add tomato, cheddar, green onions, green pepper, peas and whatever you wish to have in the salad."
  - "Mix together lemon juice, water, garlic, mustard, oil, salt, and pepper."
  - "Double, triple, or quadruple amounts as necessary for amount of pasta you are serving. Toss with pasta and refrigerate until cool."
---
