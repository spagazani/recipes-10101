tags: unclassified
title: "Homemade Bagels"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 pkg dry yeast"
      - "1 3/4 cups warm water"
      - "2 Tbsp honey"
      - "5 to 5 1/2 cups bread flour, divided"
      - "2 tsp salt"
      - "Cooking spray"
      - "1/2 cup sesame or poppy seeds, or sea salt, dried onion, or garlic flakes"
      - "1 Tbsp cornmeal"
instructions:
  - "Combine yeast and warm water in a bowl; let stand 5 minutes. Stir in honey."
  - "Combine yeast mixture, 2 cups flour and salt in a large bowl; beat at low speed of heavy-duty mixer 4 minutes or until smooth."
  - "Gradually stir in enough remaining flour to make a soft dough. Beat at medium low speed with a dough hook for 8 minutes or until smooth and elastic."
  - "Divide the dough into 12 equal portions: roll each into a smooth ball. Cover and let rest 5 minutes."
  - "Roll each ball into an 11” inch rope. Bring ends together and pinch to seal. Roll bagel around the palm of your hand to make a ring."
  - "Place on baking sheets coated with cooking spray; cover and chill 12 to 18 hours."
  - "Bring a huge pot of water to a boil. Add bagels 4 at a time and cook 30 seconds turning once. Remove with a slotted spoon and place on wire racks."
  - "Dredge bagels in sesame seeds. Preheat oven to 450F coat baking sheets with cooking spray and sprinkle with cornmeal. Place bagels on prepared sheets. Bake for 13 minutes or until golden. Makes 12 bagels."
