--- 
title: "Mom's Potato Salad"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "4 cups diced cold cooked potatoes"
      - "1 1/2 cups sliced or diced celery"
      - "2 Tbsp finely chopped onion"
      - "1 Tbsp chopped parsley"
      - "3/4 cup Miracle Whip"
      - "1 Tbsp vinegar"
      - "1 tsp prepared mustard"
      - "2 tsp salt"
      - "Pinch of pepper"
instructions:
  - "Combine potatoes, celery, onion, and parsley. Mix together the remaining ingredients and pour over the potatoes."
  - "Toss lightly and chill at least 1 hour before serving."
---
