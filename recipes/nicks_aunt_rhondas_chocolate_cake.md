title: "Nick's Aunt Rhonda's Chocolate Cake"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "2 1/2 cups flour"
      - "2 cups sugar"
      - "8oz margarine"
      - "4 eggs"
      - "1/2 tsp salt"
      - "1/2 cup cocoa powder"
      - "3 tsp baking powder"
      - "1 cup milk"
instructions:
  - "Sift flour twice, then sift flour again adding cocoa, baking powder and salt."
  - "Cream butter and sugar, then add eggs one at a time to sugar mixture. Add dry ingredients with milk."
  - "Divide the mixture between two 9” cake pans that have been greased and floured."
  - "Bake at 350F for 30 minutes. Decorate with purchased frosting or make some of your own."
