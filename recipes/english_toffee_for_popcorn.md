--- 
title: "English Toffee For Popcorn"
ingredients:
  - section: "ALL"
    items:
      - "1 cup sugar"
      - "1/3 cup butter"
      - "1/4 cup vinegar"
instructions:
  - "Bring all ingredients to a boil in a large pot."
  - "When mixture starts to turn colour, remove from heat immediately and pour over popped popcorn."
  - "Stir well and enjoy."
---
